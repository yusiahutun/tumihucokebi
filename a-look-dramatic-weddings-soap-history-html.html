A look at the most dramatic weddings in soap history

July 25, 2024
blog

Soap fans love nothing more than a good wedding, as they typically set the stage for plenty of drama.

Whether it be an unexpected tragedy, lies being exposed, jilting or a chapel brawl, the announcement of impending nuptials means drama is never far away.

Next week looks set to be no different, as Coronation Street's Daisy Midgeley and Daniel Osborne's wedding day finally arrives, despite the constant threat of her deranged stalker Justin.

Bosses have already confirmed that Daisy will be targeted by an acid attack, sparking doubt around whether she will make it down the aisle.

So, as viewers eagerly await the big day on Monday, here is a look at some of the most dramatic weddings in soap history...

Bombshell! From Roxy and Ronnie Mitchell's watery end (pictured), to baby bombshells and epic disasters... MailOnline takes a lookback at some of soap's most explosive weddings

Devastating: Fans of the iconic sisters were devastated when Ronnie and Roxy Mitchell met an unexpected end mere hours after tying the knot with Jack Branning on New Year's Day in 2017

EastEnders - Roxy and Ronnie Mitchell's post-wedding drowning

Fans of the iconic sisters were devastated when Ronnie Mitchell met an unexpected end mere hours after tying the knot with Jack Branning on New Year's Day in 2017.

After a life plagued by turmoil and tragedy viewers were stunned when she drowned after diving into a swimming pool to save her sister Roxy.

Chatting and laughing together, a drunk Roxy decided to enjoy a late night dip in the pool - seconds after ominously stating that it was 'the end of an era'. 

Plunging into the water, it soon became clear to viewers that the troubled character had become motionless - causing Ronnie to leap in after her.

However still clad in her wedding dress and veil, the newlywed began to struggle against the material, when viewers heard a spine-tingling scream from under the water as she began to drown.

Adding to the dramatic and heartbreaking effect, the sequence was played out beside Jack reading the story of Cinderella to the kids as he waited for his wife to return.

It already been confirmed by both stars that they would be leaving the soap, but it still devastated many fans, who were disappointed by their eventual end.

Coronation Street - Eva Price's baby lies are exposed

Devastating: Coronation Street's Eva Price was exposed on her wedding day, after lying to her cheating fiancé Aidan that she was pregnant with his baby

After discovering her boyfriend Aidan Connor as cheating on her with Maria, a furious Eva swore revenge.

Proposing to Aidan, she vowed to spend every penny he had on their lavish wedding, before taking things further by leading him to believe she was pregnant with his baby.

Using fake scans she found online, Eva convinced Aidan that he was going to be a father, and eventually the pair made it to their wedding day.

However, Maria discovered Eva's lies and planned to expose the bride while she was at the altar, leaving Aidan devastated.

The dramatic scenes culiminated with Eva and Maria having a huge fist fight in the fountain bogged down by her enormous wedding dress, and it soon became clear her relationship with Aidan couldn't be saved.

Emmerdale - Chas exposes Carl's affair

Explosive: After learning Carl King had been cheating on her, Emmerdale's Chas Dingle got her revenge by exposing him on their wedding day in 2010

For years, Chas Dingle and Carl King were one of Emmerdale's most long-running couples, but things fell apart in 2010 when he began an affair with Eve Jenson.

Thanks to a well-placed bra, Chas discovered Carl's infidelity, but rather than dump him, she proposed, and led him to believe she was splashing out thousands on her big day.

With the help of hideously-dressed bridesmaids, Eve included, Chas made it to the altar with Carl, but when asked to recite her vows, she refused, declaring: 'I won't be faithful because you haven't been faithful to me!'

Exposing Carl's affair to the entire village, a furious Chas slapped Eve across the face before storming out of the church, struggling to hide her upset that her relationship was over.

Sadly she and Carl were unable to repair their romance, and things turned nasty between the pain when he discovered she'd kept all the money he'd given her to spend on the wedding.

Coronation Street - Karen discovers Steve's fling with Tracy 

Drama! In a scene that's gone down as one of the most iconic in Corrie, Karen's big day ended in disaster, when she learned Steve was the husband of Tracy's daughter Amy

In a scene that's gone down as one of the most iconic in Corrie history, Karen McDonald's big day ended in disaster, when she learned her husband-to-be Steve was the father of Tracy's daughter Amy.

Tracy crashed the big day having previously led the village to believe that Roy Cropper was the child's father, but while being dragged out of the church blurted out the truth. 

In an explosive moment, Karen furiously turned to Tracy when she discovered the bombshell, before threatening her with a stiletto heel.

Somehow, Steve convinced Karen that Tracy meant nothing to him, and they went ahead with the wedding, though in typical soap fashion, it didn't last.

EastEnders - Stella's abuse of Ben is revealed

Drama! Phil Mitchell was furious when he learned Stella Crawford had been abusing his son Ben, and a tense rooftop showdown resulted in her death

Having been unlucky in love, it appeared Phil Mitchell had finally found his dream partner in lawyer Stella Crawford.

However, while the pair went full steam ahead with their wedding plans, viewers learned she had been secretly abusing his son Ben, and it all came to a head on the big day.

Ben finally plucked up the courage to tell Phil what Stella had done, after she threatened him and warned him to keep quiet.

To no surprise, Phil was enraged when he discovered what Stella had done, and as she fled the venue, he followed in hot pursuit.

It culminated in a dramatic rooftop showdown, where a deranged Stella threatened to jump unless Phil said he loved her.

But instead, he insisted he decided to marry her because she was 'easy and safe,' and in a surprise cliffhanger, it was revealed Stella had fallen from the roof to her death.

While the ending sparked questions about whether Stella was pushed, or jumped, Phil soon revealed she committed suicide by leaping from the roof to meet her fate.

Emmerdale - Debbie and Pete's helicopter tragedy

Huge! In one of Emmerdale's most explosive plots, Debbie Dingle and Pete Barton's wedding ended in tragedy, when a malfunctioning helicopter crashed into the venue

Heartbreaking: An epic helicopter tragedy on Debbie and Pete's wedding day resulted in the deaths of two major characters, including Ruby Haswell

In one of Emmerdale's most explosive plots, Debbie Dingle and Pete Barton's wedding ended in tragedy, when a malfunctioning helicopter crashed into the venue.

The disaster had been caused thanks to a car explosion which sent a gas tank flying into the air and hit the copter, sending it crashing into the ground.

The episodes had already proven dramatic as Pete learned Debbie had been cheating on him with his brother Ross, but his rage was short-lived when much of the village was trapped under the rubble. 

The tense storyline led to the deaths of two major characters, first Ruby Haswell, who died at the scene after suffering a severe laceration to her stomach.

Val Pollard was trapped in a fairground mirror maze by the blast with Diane Sugden, and before she could be rescued, was struck by a falling shard of glass.

In one of soap's more epic departures, Val met her maker with a cigarette in hand, declaring: 'I'm H.I.Val. I'm Valerie Pollard of the Valerie Pollard foundation... I'm Val!'

Viewers were also led to believe that Pete killed his brother Ross in a fight afollowing the affair bombshell, after he hit his head on a concrete wall.

While Pete attempted to bury Ross' body, it was later revealed that Ross had survived the ordeal.
