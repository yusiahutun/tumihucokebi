It's unusual for wedding photos to really capture the internet's imagination unless the bride or groom in question are celebrites, but the nuptial snapshots of this duo were so breathtaking that they did just that.

Nell Diamond, daughter of former Barclays chief Bob Diamond, married Ted Wasserman, a managing director at Deutsche Bank, at a fairytale ceremony in the South of France this weekend.

The photos were posted on Instagram under the hashtag #nellandteddy, and it didn't take long for people to marvel at their picture-perfect appeal.
