Goo Goo Dolls Announce 20th Anniversary Tour

September 15, 2024
blog

Goo Goo Dolls I Bob Mussel

LOS ANGELES, CA (CelebrityAccess) – This Fall, multi-platinum Grammy-nominated band Goo Goo Dolls will embark on the SiriusXM Presents: Goo Goo Dolls – Dizzy Up The Girl 20th Anniversary Tour, in celebration of the 20th anniversary of their iconic album, Dizzy Up The Girl.

First released on September 22, 1998, via Warner Bros. Records, Dizzy Up The Girl, catapulted the Goo Goo Dolls to mainstream success. Having since sold 6 million copies worldwide and been certified 4x platinum in the US, Dizzy Up The Girl is one of the band’s most succesful albums to date.

Set to perform the album in its entirety at each show once the tour kicks off in Phoenix, AZ on September 30th, fans can catch the band on one of their more than 20 + dates across the US before they wrap things up in Las Vegas, NV on November 18th.

Fan Club and VIP Package pre-sales begin on Tuesday, April 10 at 10:00AM local time. Tickets will go on sale to the general public on Friday, April 13 at 10:00AM local time. For more information on VIP Packages and tickets, visit GooGooDolls.com.
