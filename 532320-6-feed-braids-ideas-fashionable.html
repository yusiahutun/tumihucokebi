6 feed in braids to the back ideas that are fashionable

Hair braiding has revolutionised. At one point, cornrows and plaits were a form of political expression and were only rocked by a few. Today, braids are popular, and better styles have been invented. The 6 feed-in braids to the back ideas are best for people looking for a more natural look and protective option.

What is the feed in braids hairstyle? This braiding style encompasses "feeding" the extensions into the natural hair close to the roots. The braids start small at the beginning of the row and widen as they advance towards the neck nape as more hair is added.

6 feed in braids to the back hairstyles

Are you looking for chic 6 feed in braid hairstyles to make you stand out? Here are some incredible 6 feed in braid hairstyles that are stylish and will easily transition you from a casual to a professional look.

1. 6 Large feed in stitch cornrows

This hairstyle involves skilfully creating six distinct, large cornrow braids. The technique gradually adds extensions as the braids progress, providing a sleek, natural look. These 6 large cornrows aren't only functional in keeping the hair neatly secured and serve as a stylish and eye-catching element.

2. 6 feed in braids to the back with curls

In this hairstyle, the braids elegantly culminate into luscious curls that add glamour. Fusing braids and curls enhances the hairstyle's dimension and texture and provides a trendy and protective option. This results in a great combination of flair and precision.

3. 6 feed in braids to the back with beads

Adding beads in the 6 feed in braids enhances the overall hairstyle aesthetics, offering a decorative and playful element. The beads should be strategically placed along each braid length, or you can ask them to be installed as you like for a touch of individuality.

4. 6 feed in braids to the back with weave

You can incorporate weaves in your beautiful feed in braids to add volume and length, enhancing the overall hairstyle texture. The result will be a seamless, protective, sophisticated, and chic hairstyle. The overall look embodies practicality and aesthetic appeal.

5. 6 feed in braid mid back with curly ends

The braids perfectly flow down the back, reaching mid-length with impressive symmetry. What makes them distinctive are the enchanting curly ends gracing each braid, adding a playful element to the look.
