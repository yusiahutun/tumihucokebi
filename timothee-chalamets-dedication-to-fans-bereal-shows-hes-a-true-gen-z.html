Timothee Chalamet's dedication to fans BeReal shows he's a true Gen Z

July 27, 2024
blog

Timothee Chalamet took to the Leicester Square red carpet in London last night for the Wonka premiere, but he wasn’t going anywhere until he’d taken the perfect BeReal for one lucky fan.

The Hollywood star is starring as Willy Wonka in Paul King’s new movie, and his antics at the world premiere had fans loving him even more than they already do if that was possible.

Timothee Chalamet hailed the ‘GOAT’ as he helps fan take BeReal

The premiere was filled with hundreds of fans hoping to get a selfie with their celebrity idol, however, one fan got more than she bargained for.

In a video, now going viral on TikTok, one fan asked if the actor, rumored to be dating Kylie Jenner, could star in her BeReal. However, he didn’t just take one quick selfie and go.

“Let’s do a funny awkward one,” he told the fan before ‘awkwardly’ staring into space.

However, the fan wasn’t happy with her part of the BeReal, which turned out blurry. Chalamet agreed to take it again, not like he had a whole premiere to attend to or anything…

Once again the fan’s camera went weird so she asked to take it a third time, and Timothee continued to pose awkwardly with no fuss.

Fans have taken to the comments of the adorable video, and they now love him even more.

“I love the fact he stayed there until you got the perfect picture,” penned one.

“This has single-handedly made me like Timothee Chalamet a lot more,” said another.

“Three attempts?? Man’s got patience,” one fan joked.

One wrote to the creator: “I know you were stressing so bad,” to which she responded: “the fact it didn’t show on the screen record is insane because of the way I was FUMING on the inside.” Honestly, we would be too.

The star doesn’t disappoint with his outfit

Once again, Chalamet channeled his inner Willy Wonka, this time opting for a pink Tom Ford womenswear suit.

The 27-year-old didn’t feel the need to wear a shirt underneath, but he didn’t hold back on the accessories. Around his neck was a Cartier custom necklace, which according to British Vogue took 450 hours to make.

The popping colors seem to be a running theme for the star, as at the Tokyo premiere, he wore a bright lilac, ‘leather’ jumpsuit.
