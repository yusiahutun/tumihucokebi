Chris Stapleton and Wife Morgane Stapleton Are Unrecognizable in This Photo

March 03, 2024
blog
  • Chris Stapleton is known for his country hits, but the singer's iconic appearance is just as easy to spot.
  • He is completely unrecognizable without long hair in these throwback photos, some of which were shared by his wife Morgane Stapleton.

Country star Chris Stapleton has spent most of his career behind the scenes. After moving to Nashville in 2001, the artist found success writing hit songs for other artists. He has penned major hits for Miranda Lambert, Tim McGraw, Adele, Luke Bryan, Brad Paisley, and more. Then, after a show-stopping duet with Justin Timberlake at the November 2015 CMA Awards, Chris quickly became a household name. Now, the Kentucky native is known not only for his prolific lyrics, but also for his own voice.

Taylor Hill//Getty Images

Chris and Morgane in 2017.

The only thing more recognizable than Chris's vocals is his appearance. He looks exactly how you'd envision a country crooner would: his wavy, light brown hair is barely shorter than his wife's, his untamed beard falls at about the same length as his mane, and all of this is usually topped off with a cowboy hat. It's a look so iconic that it's hard imagine Chris wasn't born this way. But there was a time when the singer was much more clean-cut—and we have the photos to prove it.

Earlier this year, Chris's wife Morgane Stapleton shared a throwback photo of the couple that made our jaws drop. She hardly looks different, but her husband is not the same person.

This shocking picture inspired us to dig a little deeper into the singer's past, where we found even more evidence that a clean-shaven Chris once existed.

Back in 2013, the country star actually acknowledged his transformation with a cheeky caption alongside an old photo he posted.

"One of these guys is me," Chris wrote.

It's true: he's the person on the far right. We couldn't believe it either!
