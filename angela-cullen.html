Angela Cullen is a High-Performance Physiotherapist and Performance Trainer located in New Zealand with UK expertise. She has over 15 years of expertise in the sectors of sports and physiotherapy. Cullen’s work as a performance trainer began after she completed her four-year bachelor’s degree at famed Great Britain 100/200-meter sprinters. She worked for the prominent English Institute of Sport (EIS) for roughly six years (2000 to 2006). Angela was a Senior Physiotherapist at EIS, a London-based institute that improves Olympic, Paralympic, and non-Olympic sports performance via medicine, technology, engineering, and science.

Angela Cullen joined the High-Performance Sports and Recreation (SPARC) NZ team as Senior Performance Advisor in 2006 and remained for four years.
