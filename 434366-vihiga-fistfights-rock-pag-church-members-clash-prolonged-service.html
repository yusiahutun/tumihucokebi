Chaos ensued in the Pentecostal Assemblies of God-Kenya (PAG-K) in Sabatia, Vihiga county between two warring sides over a service that took longer than expected.

It is reported that the church is divided into two factions, one rallying behind the pastor and another one dubbed the 'caretaker' which has its own leader.

The two groups worship in the same church but in shifts, Citizen reported.

During the Sunday, November 21, service, the caretaker's group is said to have worshipped longer than expected, a move that irked the pastor's group which was waiting outside.
