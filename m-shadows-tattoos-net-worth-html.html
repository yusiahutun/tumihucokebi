What Do M. Shadows's Tattoos Tell?

Once a blank canvas, his body now has countless tattoos that are fairly relevant to his life. His ink journey started when he was 15 years of age, and the journey has continued ever since. 

M. Shadows also, Matthew Sanders never had any plans of inking his face, but he had always been keen to cover everything apart from that.

Shadows were heavily influenced by other triumphant bands in the industry, such as Guns N' Roses and Slipknot.

Also as a reason of which the band has been able to perform as well as they have, not to mention other skilled artists of his band. 

The band together has put out nine albums since 2001.

The start of their journey was with the album, Sounding the Seventh Trumpet. The band put out their latest album back in 2018, named 'Black Reign.'

Going into the nitty-gritty of M. Shadows's net worth, he has a luxurious house in Huntington Beach, California (CA), US.
