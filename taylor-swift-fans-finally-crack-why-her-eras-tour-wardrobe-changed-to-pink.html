Taylor Swift fans finally crack why her Eras Tour wardrobe changed to pink

February 17, 2024
blog

Tay-Tay’s Eras Tour is one of the most talked about concert tours to grace our planet, and her wardrobe, song list and choreographer tends to make headlines. But now Taylor Swift fans finally work out why she changed all of her Eras Tour outfits to pink.

The 34-year-old singer is enjoying the next leg of her tour in Singapore with six shows in South East Asia. Her beau Travis Kelce even managed to set time aside in his schedule to jet-set across the globe to support her. Swifties have been loving the content on social media of the music star. Whether it’s her time on stage or enjoying a date night with Travis at a mall. However, Taylor Swift fans now realize why she made a drastic – pink – change to her Eras Tour wardrobe yesterday.

Taylor Swift wears pink on Eras Tour

One of her outfits from the concert fans love is her bodysuit for the Lover era, and she switched it up from blue, and teal and gold, to dark and hot pink. And you know she also switched out her knee-high boots that go with it too.

Then for the 1989 era, she switched up her two piece of a mini skirt and crop bar to a deep pink, covered in sequins and embellishments.

Taylor Swift fans particularly enjoyed her sparkling pink dress for the Speak Now portion of the Eras Tour setlist.

Hanging down to the floor, the slim-strapped gown is ideal for any fairytale – something she may be feeling as part of the biggest celebrity couples on the planet.

Yesterday, March 8, was International Women’s Day. A special annual event to celebrate women’s achievements in politics, business, education and all areas, as well as raise awareness over inequality issues and equal rights.

Taylor, a proud feminist, appeared to switch up her outfits to celebrate the day herself while on tour.

Fans love her supporting the important day

Finally, the penny dropped. Not that Swifties were upset over the wardrobe change, Taylor looks great in any fashion number.

One wrote on Twitter: “OHHHHH that explains all the pink.”

A second penned: “All so beautiful. Happy international women’s day,” followed by someone else who simply said: “I like that she did that.”

While a third realized: “Wait the folklore dress looks so pretty here.”

However, one fan wished they had seen the future: “Ugh I should have thought of that when I made my outfit picks.”
