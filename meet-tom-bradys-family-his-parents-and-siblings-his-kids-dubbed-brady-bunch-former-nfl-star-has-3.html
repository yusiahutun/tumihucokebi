In case you were curious, here’s what we know about Tom Brady’s family life:

Who are Tom Brady’s parents?

Tom Brady is the son of his supportive parents Tom Brady Senior, 79, and Galynn Patricia Brady, 78. The Brady parents have been married for over 55 years, since 1969, and they’ve encouraged their son throughout his career.

“When you think about … the commitment that parents make and that my parents made to me and my career is unbelievable,” he shared during a 2022 episode of his podcast, Let’s Go with Tom Brady, Larry Fitzgerald and Jim Gray.

According to People, Tom Sr runs an insurance firm called Thomas Brady & Associates, which appears to have offices in California, Boston, New York City and Michigan. His mother, meanwhile, worked as a flight attendant with TWA.

Who are Tom Brady’s sisters?

Tom is the youngest of four siblings. He has three sisters: Maureen, Julie and Nancy, and their family has affectionately been dubbed the “Brady bunch” by fans.

Maureen, Tom’s oldest sister, was born in 1970, and played collegiate softball at Fresno State University, according to The Eagle-Tribune, and was later inducted into the San Mateo County Sports Hall of Fame.

Julie, born in 1974, is often mistaken for being Tom’s twin, as they look very alike. She too played sports at university and ended up marrying a former professional baseball player named Kevin Youkilis, per People.

In 1976, Tom’s parents welcomed Nancy, who also got the sporty gene and scored a scholarship to play softball at UC Berkeley, per People.

Having such an athletic family made the Bradys competitive. “I started it,” Tom Senior told The Eagle-Tribune. “Everything we did, and I mean everything, like running home from church, throwing a rock the farthest … Everything was a competition. I guess it made things really fun, at least for the winner.”

They grew up in California

According to People, Tom and his siblings were raised in San Mateo, California.

Despite their son’s astronomical success as a professional athlete, the Brady parents reportedly still live in the same house where they raised their kids, per The Eagle-Tribune.

They are one big family

The Brady bunch has continued to expand as the years have gone by.

In fact, per People, Tom Senior and Galynn are proud grandparents to a whopping 10 grandchildren in total – which would surely make Thanksgivings and Christmases very busy!
