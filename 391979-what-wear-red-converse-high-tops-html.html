What to wear with red Converse high tops

Do you own a pair of red Converse high tops and you're wondering what goes well with it? A pair of red Converse represents a bold statement with an amazing fun pop of colour. These shoes will not only add a dynamic flair to your outfit but are also super comfy. However, you must be careful what you wear with red converse high tops to avoid looking funky or like a clown.

It is relatively easy to style black high top Converse than red ones due to its vibrant colour. Nevertheless, a pair of red Converse high tops is versatile and can complement various outfits from gym wear to smart casual and even a classic look. With endless styling options available, it becomes somewhat tricky to decide.

What to wear with red Converse high tops

A pair of red Converse draws enough attention to your feet, therefore, pairing it up with coloured and lighter colours will help balance the entire look. Additionally, the design and colour allow these comfortable shoes to match with almost anything.

How to wear high top converse for girls

What looks good with red Converse? Since red is bright, dark coloured outfits will never go wrong with red Converse high tops. Stay away from monochromatic colours and bright hues. Here are some red high top Converse outfit ideas for girls

1. Denim dress, shorts, or pair of trousers

Denim is arguably the most versatile fashion accessory that has ever existed and goes so well with red Converse. Interestingly, any shade of Denim from light to dark can work well with the pop of red. Also, any Denim outfit, such as a pair of trousers, dresses, skirts, or shorts, go well with red shoes. So do not be shy pair it up with your high top converse.

2. High top converse with skinny jeans and black leather jacket

Regarding how to wear high top converse with skinny jeans, try pairing your skinny denim jeans with a black leather jacket. If the jeans are long, you can opt to tuck in your jeans into your shoes or let your pants cover the shoes.

3. Black or white tops with red Converse high top

Besides Denim, neutral colours like black and white are excellent styling options for red Converse. For example, a white t-shirt, or jumper and black or blue skinny jeans will give you a timeless and classic look.

If you are a skirt lover, pair up your high waisted skirt with a tank top and do not forget to tuck it in. This is a lovely outfit idea suited for a day or night look. Mixing up your red Converse with black, white and Denim will make you stand out.

3. Red Converse high tops with green outfits

Nothing beats the magical combination of red and green as the two colors complement well when properly matched. The best shades of green to match outfits with red converse high tops include darker tones like forest or olive and lighter ones like lime green.

Interestingly, a completely red outfit will work perfectly with your red Converse, similar to when you tweak it with a few red details to look exquisite.
