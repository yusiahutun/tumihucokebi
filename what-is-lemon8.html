What Is Lemon8 and How Does It Work?

blog

Lemon8 is a social media app that has been making waves online, especially on TikTok, where many influencers have been promoting it as a cross between Instagram and Pinterest. But what is Lemon8 exactly, and how does it work?

Read on to find answers to these questions and more.

What Is Lemon8?

Lemon8 is a photo and video-sharing platform. It was launched by ByteDance, the parent company of TikTok, in Japan in 2020 and then expanded to other Asian countries like Thailand and Singapore. It debuted in the US and the UK in February 2023 but took off in March after media organizations and TikTok influencers began promoting it.

According to its App Store description, Lemon8 is "a place for young creatives to share a diversity of content from fashion, makeup, food, and travel, to homewares, pets, and anything else you can imagine". Several TikTok influencers have also described the app as a cross of Instagram (before Reels) and Pinterest.

Lemon8 is similar to TikTok in that it has both a "Following" and a "For You" page, but that's just about where the similarities end. Lemon8 is more focused on photos than videos, which is a huge relief considering the TikTok-ification of most other social media platforms.

The app allows users to create posts using templates, stickers, filters, and fonts. It also has a carousel feature that lets users upload multiple photos in one post.

How Does Lemon8 Work?

Close

Lemon8 works similarly to other social media apps. You sign up with your email or phone number, choose your username and pronouns, and select your interests. Then, you can start browsing the app and interacting with other users.

There are two main sections in the app: Following and For You. The Following section shows posts from the users you follow, while the For You section shows those that match your interests. You can also switch between different categories under the For You section by tapping the category name at the top of the screen.

How to Use Lemon8

Close

To use Lemon8, you first have to download the app from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store and create an account.

Once you have set up your profile, you can start creating and sharing content on Lemon8. To create a post, tap on the plus (+) icon at the bottom of the screen and choose whether you want to upload photos or videos from your camera roll or take new ones using the app's camera. You can then edit your photos or videos using the app's tools and add a caption with hashtags.

To explore other users' content on Lemon8, scroll the For You feed or tap any category that interests you. You can also use the search function to look for specific hashtags or keywords. You can like, comment, or share any post that you find interesting by tapping on the icons below each post.

To follow other users on Lemon8, you can tap on their username or profile picture and then tap the Follow button on their profile page. You can also see who they are following and who follows them by tapping on the numbers next to their username.

Should You Download Lemon8 Yet?

If you're looking to jump on a new social media platform, Lemon8 is worth checking out. The app offers a unique blend of Instagram and Pinterest-style content that's been gaining popularity, and the app's tools are easy to use.

However, the app is much younger than its competitors, so there's far less content and engagement than on other leading social media platforms. If you're looking for a platform with more user engagement, you may want to wait and see how Lemon8 develops.
