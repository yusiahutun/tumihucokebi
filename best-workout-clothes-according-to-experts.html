The Best Workout Clothes, According to Fitness Experts

May 14, 2024
blog

"I discovered Gymshark right before the pandemic. I remember slipping on Gymshark leggings and being obsessed with the material and the fit. They had a few different collections and I started trying them all, but I noticed I kept coming back to the Vital Seamless collection.

"The way the leggings moved with my body when working out is what made me fall in love. Whether I'm running, squatting heavy, or jumping around for HIIT workouts, I know that I don't have to worry about my pants falling or being see-through. It's also designed with a sweat-wick material, which is a lifesaver for a heavy sweater like me. The Vital Seamless bra is also a special piece, because it is a challenge to find double-D-friendly sports bras. While the bra is a medium-support one, I find myself doubling up on it and I'm fine throughout my workouts. Because the material is so lightweight, I never have to worry about feeling like I am suffocating under two sports bras.

"I have the Vital Seamless sets in almost every color, and my favorite way to wear them is pairing complementing colors. It's also my airport uniform, because I feel so confident in it and it's so flattering on my body."
