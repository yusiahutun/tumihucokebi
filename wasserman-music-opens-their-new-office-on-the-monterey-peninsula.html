Wasserman Music Opens Their New Office On The Monterey Peninsula

September 16, 2024
blog

CARMEL-BY-THE-SEA, California (CelebrityAccess) — Wasserman Music announced the debut of the agency’s newest office in Carmel-by-the-Sea, California.

Located on California’s Monterey Bay, the office continues Team Wass’s longstanding connection with the region that dates back to the days of Monterey Peninsula Artists, which was acquired by Paradigm in 2005 and which was in turn acquired by Wasserman in 2021.

The office, which is located in the heart of Carmel-By-The-Sea’s downtown, will serve as home base for Wasserman Music’s senior vice President Dan Weiner, vice president Lynn Cingari, vice president of colleges Taylor Shultz, and coordinator Dave Jackson.

“We are excited to open our new @WassermanMusic office in Carmel-by-the-Sea, California! Our music team’s connection to the Monterey Bay area stretches back to the founding of Monterey Peninsula Artists in 1975, and we’re thrilled to unveil our beautiful new home there to continue that lineage,” Wasserman Music said, announcing the new office.
