Best London hair salons - Top London hairdressers

blog

There are countless reasons to visit the buzzy Beauty Club London, which you’ll find tucked away behind Oxford Street. Despite the busy bustle of this shopping district, you wouldn't know it once you're inside. Beauty Club London is the location for the works: inside you'll find two nail bars, a fully equipped cocktail bar, plus it's home to Edy Kurowska who offers the most impeccable lash extensions in town - tried and trusted by Bazaar.

But it’s also a highly rated hair salon - we particularly recommend balayage with in-house pro Shady Harb, who knows exactly how to create the tones you're looking for whatever your hair colour. His work is speedy, though precise, and creates natural looks with minimal damage.

Beauty Club London is also home to hair extensions specialist, Louise Bailey, and her low-maintenance technique for adding length or volume to the natural hair is quick but transformative. It’s also addictive, though thankfully the tape-in hair extensions are reusable, and can be easily repositioned as the hair grows.

For more information, visit beautyclublondon.com
