Where was Hollie Strano born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, Education

Hollie Strano was born in Lyndhurst, Cleveland, Ohio, on December 3, 1972. She is of American descent and of Caucasian descent. Strano grew up with her two siblings, Stephanie Strano and Mike Strano, in Lyndhurst, New Jersey.

Strano attended Saint Clare Grade School in Lyndhurst. She subsequently enrolled at Notre Dame Cathedral High School. She subsequently matriculated at John Carroll University, from which she graduated with a bachelor’s degree.

Hollie also attended Mississippi State University for Agriculture and Applied Sciences, where she earned a Science degree with a meteorology concentration. Hollie remarried after three years; her second spouse is a mixologist from Buffalo named Alex Giangreco. According to reports, they met in 2010 in Fort Lauderdale, Florida, which was also the site of their nuptials. However, the relationship did not succeed, and in 2017 they divorced.
