Meaning of Love You Always (Starfire) [Purps Remix] by Juice WRLD

blog

The Juice WRLD song titled 'Love You Always (Starfire) [Purps Remix]' explores the theme of profound and unending love. It delves into the emotions and experiences associated with being deeply in love with someone, and the lengths one is willing to go for that person. The lyrics of the song convey the intensity, vulnerability, and complexities of love.One standout lyric in the song is, "Even when I'm asleep, I feel awake when you're away." This lyric highlights the idea that love can transcend distance and time. It suggests that even in the absence of the person they are in love with, they still feel connected and alive. This theme of longing and yearning for someone even in their absence is a common thread in the song.Another significant lyric is, "Almost left my side the other day, it makes me shake, knowing you were gon' walk away, you can't be replaced." This lyric demonstrates the fear of losing someone they deeply love and the realization that the person is irreplaceable. It explores the vulnerability and uncertainty that comes with love, where the fear of abandonment is a constant presence. It also taps into the belief that the love they have for this person is unique and unmatched, making it impossible to replace them in their life.The lyric, "For your love, workin' all day, fuck off day" conveys the idea of dedicating oneself entirely to the person they love. It expresses the willingness to put in the effort and work required to nurture and maintain the relationship. It demonstrates the theme of commitment and sacrifice for the sake of love, where no day off is taken from loving and caring for their partner."Teen Titan, got the Starfire by my side," is another lyric that adds dimension to the song's theme. It references the DC superhero character Starfire, known for her fiery and passionate nature. This lyric symbolizes the presence of a strong and supportive partner, who brings light and warmth into the singer's life. It depicts the idea that love can empower and inspire individuals to be their best selves, just as Starfire does for the Teen Titans.The song also delves into the themes of growth and transformation. The lyric, "And it changed my life, I'm tryna get close with the stars tonight," illustrates the transformative power of love. It suggests that being in love has had a profound impact on the singer's life, leading them to strive for greater heights and forge a connection with something greater than themselves. It symbolizes the idea that love can propel individuals towards personal growth and self-discovery.In summary, the song 'Love You Always (Starfire) [Purps Remix]' by Juice WRLD is an exploration of intense and forever love. Through various lyrics, it touches upon themes such as longing, vulnerability, commitment, and transformation. Overall, the song depicts love as a powerful force that can transcend boundaries and inspire individuals to reach for the stars in pursuit of their deep and everlasting love.
