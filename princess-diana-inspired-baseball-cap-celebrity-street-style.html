Every supermodel is copying Princess Diana's favourite street style accessory right now

March 17, 2024
blog

The baseball cap is having a moment

    Forget achingly chic It-bags and dreamy footwear, the accessory that seemingly every A-lister is rocking right now is the humble baseball cap. Hat trends come and go, but the staple cap is something of a timeless classic.

    Royal style icon Princess Diana couldn't have predicted the lasting impact that her chic athleisure ensembles would come to have on the modern street style – currently #princessdianastyle boasts an impressive 44.3m views on TikTok.

    Avid fans of the princess' outfits will recall that she strongly favoured graphic sweatshirts, cycling shorts and chunky trainers in the late 1980s and early 1990s. Even when nipping to the gym, she made her style a priority – as if we couldn't love her any more.

    The princess often wore a navy baseball cap 

    One of her most-adored accessories was a navy camp adorned with gold embroidery. The princess wore it constantly, styling it with a blazer and mid-calf brown leather boots at school drop off as well as with jeans and her iconic balloon sweatshirt at the polo. Versatile – we very much approve.

    The baseball cap is having a moment right now and many recent converts to Princess Diana's off-duty accessory staple are of the supermodel variety.

    Rhode founder Hailey Bieber rocked a red New York Yankees cap 

    Hailey Bieber, who has publicly cited the princess as one of her personal style icons, has been spotted for the second time this month sporting a red New York Yankees cap. Both times the supermodel-cum-skinfluencer has styled her headgear with her beloved oversized leather jacket, and on her most recent outing she rocked a black crop top, relaxed-fit mid-waist jeans, brassy gold earrings and a classic belt.

    READ: 9 best baseball caps for women: Channel Princess Diana's 90s off-duty vibe

    Kendall Jenner styled hers with split hem flares 

    Like her pal, Kendall Jenner has also been flying the flag for the Yankees cap, although she opted for the princess' favoured hue, rich navy, for her baseball hat moment. The 27-year-old rocked hers with split hem flares and a Princess Diana footwear favourite: chunky dad trainers.

    Supermodel Emily Ratajkowski has also been spotted in the classic hat style 

    Another stylish celeb who has been championing the baseball cap is Emily Ratajkowski. The author styled hers with a dark blue fitted shirt and a wraparound mini skirt and a shoulder bag by cult-adored Loewe.
