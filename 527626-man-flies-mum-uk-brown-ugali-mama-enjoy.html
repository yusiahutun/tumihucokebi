In a related story, a man surprised his 74-year-old guardian with his first-ever flight to Nairobi.

In the emotional story that he narrated on his social media platforms, Mutembe recounted that when his dad died, her mother was left to single-handedly take care of him and his siblings, a burden that weighed her down.

She would spend a lot of time away from the children working, and in 1997, she took the man to a boarding school, but the mother rarely got time to visit him.
