See Beyonc Look Gorgeous in a Flowing Neon Green Gown and Attached Hood

August 27, 2024
blog

Beyoncé debuted yet another jaw-dropping monochromatic look at her latest Renaissance World Tour performance.

The superstar shared pictures of her opulent neon-green outfit this morning, after taking the stage for the first show of a three-day stop in Atlanta. The one-shouldered draped gown, which was pulled from Gaurav Gupta's fall 2023 couture collection, consisted of an artful bodice made of draped fabric with strategic sheer panels, and a flowing skirt with an open hip-high slit. A matching attached hood covered the back of the singer's head, covering her honey-blonde hair which was styled in glamorous curls.

She completed the look with matching green ankle-strap heeled sandals, a silver bone cuff bracelet by Tiffany & Co., and an array of silver rings. Her makeup included shimmery green and gold eyeshadow and a matte nude lip.

The "Cuff It" singer also wore a custom look by the Indian designer while performing in Charlotte, North Carolina, on Wednesday. The sparkling outfit consisted of a sculptural, crystal-covered bodysuit with swirling fabric along the bodice and an extended one-shouldered neckline, as well as matching legging boots. She topped off the look with a wide-brimmed, silver metallic hat, as well as a pair of diamond cat-eye sunglasses.

Several of the multi-hyphenate's standout tour looks so far have utilized vibrant color, from her sparkling Barbiecore ensembles to her monochromatic jumpsuits. She has worked with numerous luxury brands for her luxe custom looks, including Alexander McQueen, Schiaparelli, Balmain, Fendi, and Ferragamo among others.
