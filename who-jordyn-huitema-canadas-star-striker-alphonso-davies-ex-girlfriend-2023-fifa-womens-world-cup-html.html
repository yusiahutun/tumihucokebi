Who is Jordyn Huitema, Canada's star striker - and Alphonso Davies' ex-girlfriend - at the 2023 FIFA

Canada's Jordyn Huitema is charged with leading the line for her nation at the Women's World Cup in Australia and New Zealand.

Responsibility and limelight are hardly new for the 22-year-old, who rose to prominence within the soccer, and social media, world at a much younger age. 

Huitema grew up 60mi east of Vancouver in the British Columbian city of Chilliwack -- a mere three-hour drive from Seattle. 


After her talents were noticed by the Vancouver Whitecaps at an early age, she eventually signed on with B.C.-based TSS FC Rovers for the 2018 season. 

Now, further from her home than ever before, Huitema will hope her play can push Canada to its third consecutive World Cup knockout appearance. 

The Canadian women's national team star is known for her glamorous posts on her Instagram

WHO DOES SHE PLAY FOR?

Beyond the Canadian national team, Huitema plays for National Women's Soccer League club, OL Reign, who are currently the reigning NWSL champions. 

In June 2022, she transferred from Paris Saint-Germain to the Reign, signing a two-year deal with the Seattle-based club. 

Through 11 starts for the fourth-placed team, Huitema has four goals to her name.

WHEN DID SHE MAKE HER CANADA DEBUT? 

Huitema became the third-youngest player to ever debut for the CWNT, aged 15, in 2007. 

At 16, she scored her first goal for The Canucks in a 6-0 thrashing of Costa Rica in an exhibition. 

Since, she has made 64 appearances for her nation at senior level, scoring 16 goals in all.

WORLD CUP SO FAR 

The Canucks are in a challenging group involving hosts Australia, Nigeria and newcomers, the Republic of Ireland. 

Huitema led the line for Canada in its opening game vs. Nigeria in Melbourne. Nigeria survived a penalty kick to claim a 0-0 draw in which Huitema played the entire 90 minutes. 

Next up for Canada are the Republic of Ireland Wednesday morning before a clash with the highly-fancied Australia on Monday. 

Presently, they are second in the group - with the top two advancing - tied on points with Nigeria. Australia sit atop with three points.

ALPHONSO DAVIES LINKS 

Her time with PSG - 2019-22 -  was where she shot to broader fame - both for her play and relationship with the face of the men's national team, Alphonso Davies. 

The pair had been together since September 2017 but split up in 2022. With the former Canadian power couple long split up, Huitema is dating Mariners superstar, Julio Rodriguez.

She has shared several pictures with the MLB hotshot, who signed a 14-year contract with the Seattle ball club - worth up to $400million - last August.
