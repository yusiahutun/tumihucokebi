8 of the best solid perfumes to shop now

Rose Roots Radish Rose Frankincense & Patchouli

Rose Roots Radish Rose Frankincense & Patchouli

A perfume compact fit for a sea goddess. Since the shells are sourced naturally, each one has its own unique colour markings, making it all the more special. The scented balm has a mysterious, magical air to it too, thanks to the waft of frankincense and earthy patchouli.

Givenchy Irresistible Solid Perfume

Givenchy Irresistible Solid Perfume

This ultra-sleek solid perfume houses one of Givenchy's most-loved scents, Irresistible, an upbeat, joyful composition of sweet rose and pears. The slim tube fits neatly into a minaudiere, just don't mistake it for lip balm.

Jo Malone London Fragrance Combining Palette

Jo Malone London Fragrance Combining Palette

Why settle for one when you can have two? The solid perfume duos by Jo Malone London are created based on the brand's fragrance layering philosophy, but we also love the option of having a day and evening scent to take on trips away. You can choose from a range of pre-filled palettes to suit your mood or pick n' mix your go-to Jo Malone London perfume. Refills start at £22 each.

Shiro Savon Solid Perfume

Shiro Savon Solid Perfume

This subtle scent of clean sheets is perfect for a midday refresh. The perfume base, made with soybean oil and shea butter, also doubles as a moisturising balm for cuticles and hair.

Diptyque Eau Rose Solid Perfume

Diptyque Eau Rose Solid Perfume

Dreaming of strolling through a rose garden in full bloom? Diptyque's Eau Rose will take you there. It's an outdoorsy, summertime rose that's airy, fresh and ideal for those who find traditional floral scents a touch too heavy.

Lush Shade Solid Perfume

Lush Shade Solid Perfume

Don't sleep on the fragrance range at Lush. While the store itself can be a sensorial overload, there are plenty of gems within the perfume section. Case in point: this Shade solid perfume, an intoxicating sandalwood with a hint of spice.

Glossier You Perfume Solid, £24

If the scent of cosy cashmere sweaters and freshly-laundered, feather down bedding sounds up your street, you'll love the cult favourite Glossier You. Plus, the solid format is now refillable.

Jo Loves Pomelo A Fragrance Paintbrush Gel

Jo Loves Pomelo A Fragrance Paintbrush Gel

This isn't technically a solid, but no on-the-go perfume round-up is complete without the brush-on scent pen by Jo Loves. It deposits perfume in gel form so it still sits directly on skin, without the need to stick your fingers in a pot. There are a myriad of scents to choose from, but Pomelo is especially right for summer and the zesty citrus and woody vetiver recall memories of a beachside vacation. It comes with a refill cartridge too.
