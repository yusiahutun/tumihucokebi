Chris Spell On The Bachelorette

Although working as a professional dunker may seem odd, Chris Spell, a candidate on The Bachelorette Season 20, actually makes a living doing it. He is one of the 25 men competing for Charity Lawson, the bachelorette for this season.

Chris might be the lone contender from Season 20 with a world record. He didn’t stop there either; as of 2023, he holds two Guinness World Records for jumping, the most recent of which is for “highest standing backward somersault,” which was accomplished on November 6, 2021.

For those that are interested, the height of the jump was 1.47 meters. Chris is more than simply a world record jumper, despite what his bio for The Bachelorette might suggest. On one of his Instagram profiles, he presently has over 130,000 followers, which demonstrates his significant social media following.

Chris shared some interesting details about himself with ABC, joining competitors Dotun Olubeko and Aaron Bryant among others, and let’s just say we now like him even more! The “ride or die” man mentioned that he enjoys binge-watching Grey’s Anatomy, going apple picking in the fall, and drinking wine.

Despite his love for leaping, Chris acknowledged that sports are only “a part” of who he is on March 12, 2023, when he revealed the motivation behind his personal Instagram account.
