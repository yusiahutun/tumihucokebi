Everyone wants to age like fine wine but premature signs of aging pop up and spoil the game. Luckily, the best argireline serums can help you fight against them all. Argireline is a key ingredient in these serums and it’s renowned for its anti-aging properties. 

According to a paper published in the National Library of Medicine, argireline is a non-toxic, anti-wrinkle peptide that can be considered a safe and effective alternative to Botulinum neurotoxins (BoNTs) used in cosmetic products for anti-aging benefits. Based on our observation, a serum infused with argireline is basically a botox packed in a bottle. What makes it the rockstar ingredient in serums? It aids in promoting elastin and collagen production that paves the way for plumper-looking skin. Moreover, it is a sip of water for your skin which promises moisture retention. Talking about its anti-aging benefits, it minimizes the appearance of crow’s feet, fine lines, and wrinkles. 

Moreover, these serums are armed not only with this superhero but also with a few other hydrating ingredients like hyaluronic acid, peptides, ceramides, niacinamide, algae extracts, vitamins, and other collagen-boosting goodies.

Based on our observations, some serums with argireline can be considered to be growth factor serums since they serve us with an enviable youthful radiance.

    How to Apply Argireline Serum for Maximum Results?

    Following are some steps to apply the serum for effective results.

    Step 1: Make sure you cleanse your face thoroughly with a high-grade face wash or cleanser. 

    Step 2: Once you wash off deeply-seated impurities, pat dry your skin with a micro-fiber towel gently. 

    Step 3: Apply your everyday toner.

    Step 4: Pour a pea-sized amount of the argireline serum or use the dropper/applicator for accurate application. 

    Step 5: Apply a few drops onto your cheeks, neck, forehead, and nose. Pay heed to the areas that require more TLC. 

    Step 6: Gentle pat it on your skin and wait a few minutes until the serum is fully absorbed. 

    Step 7: Follow it up with your moisturizer and then sunscreen.

    FAQ's

    What are the benefits of using argireline serums?

    Argireline serums are of great help to combat wrinkles, fine lines, crow’s feet, and other signs of aging. In addition, they work towards enhancing skin elasticity and retaining moisture.

    How should I use argireline serum and incorporate it into my skincare routine?

    You can use it during the day or night after cleansing and toning your skin.

    Can I use Argireline serum with other skincare products?

    Yes, you may use argireline serum with other skincare products for added benefits and more evident results.

    How long does it take to see results with argireline serums?

    You can see the results after a month or two, depending on your skin condition, quality of product, formulation, etc.

    Are there any side effects of using argireline serum?

    No, there are no such side effects. However, those who have extremely sensitive and dry skin might witness a little redness.

    Is argireline better than retinol?

    Yes, argireline is a quick-absorbing compound and may be considered a more effective solution for combating wrinkles and fine lines than retinol.

    Which ingredients can’t be used with argireline?

    Direct acids, LAA (L-Ascorbic Acid), and ELAA (Ethylated Ascorbic Acid) are the three ingredients that can’t be used with argireline.
