Couple to Throuple stars Lauren and Dylan are one of four existing relationships putting their romance to the test this February. Meet Couple to Throuple’s Lauren and Dylan

Lauren and Dylan Bair are a married couple taking part in the Couple to Throuple experiment.

Dylan is 32 years old during filming, and Lauren is three years his junior at 29 years old.

The couple hails from Fountain Hills, Arizona, and they’re big into fitness, judging by their Instagram pages.

Couple is welcoming a baby

After appearing on a Peacock show aiming to put their relationship to the ultimate test, it looks like Lauren and Dylan are solidifying their love for one another even further than getting married.

Taking to the ‘gram this January, they share they’re going to be “welcoming a little cub in July 2024.”

Lauren and Dylan can be seen holding up scan photos of their baby who they write is “so loved already.”

Their caption also reads: “The Universe has been providing in some unexpected ways and we can’t wait to bring this beautiful soul into our lives later this year.”
