It comes after Sophie proved reality couples can go all the way after tying the knot with her former co-star Jamie Laing.

The pair emerged from a registry office hand-in-hand in matching shades having just tied the knot in Chelsea today.

The happy couple wed at the Chelsea and Kensington registry office on the King's Road in London, where countless scenes from the reality show have been shot.

Jamie, 34, and Sophie emerged holding hands with her in a daring white dress and him in a dark suit and tie.

He was seen pumping his fist and cheering while she emerged with one hand in the air in celebration.

Guests - including Jamie and Sophie's friends, family and TV co-stars, cheered them as they walked down the steps of the registry office.

The wedding party headed off after the service for a lunch of quails eggs and steak at Stanley's, a nearby courtyard restaurant.

The Chelsea wedding is just one of two official services they will say 'I do' at, with a second ceremony to take place in Seville, Spain next month.
