Who is Taylor Tomlinson?

Taylor Tomlinson is an American stand-up comedian, television host and writer. She rose to fame after releasing three Netflix stand-up specials: Quarter-Life Crisis (2020), Look At You (2022) and Have It All (2024). She hosts the CBS late-night show After Midnight, which premiered on January 17, 2024.

Tomlinson was born on November 4, 1993, in Orange County, California, the United States of America. This means that she will be turning 31 years old in 2024.

Taylor was raised in a Christian family with her three siblings. Unfortunately, her mother died on August 14, 2002, of cancer when Taylor was eight years old. She grew up in Temecula, California, and attended Temecula Valley High School.

The comedian went to California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo and later transferred to a San Diego metropolitan area college. She also went to California State University San Marcos but dropped out to pursue her comedy career.
