David Beckham Gets Grilled on His Questionable Fashion Choices on The Late, Late Show

July 14, 2024
blog

Happy almost 18th wedding anniversary to the Beckhams!

With the big occasion quickly approaching, what’s a better time to take a trip down memory lane and look back at some of David Beckham and Victoria Beckham‘s best fashion moments?

This was James Corden‘s plan when he invited David on to his The Late, Late Show sofa — the last in a series of three to take place in the UK, rather than LA.

MORE: A Spice Girls Feud! Mel B Is “Livid” With Fellow Spice Girls Star Victoria Beckham

During their sit-down, James amusingly quizzed the soccer player on his and his wife’s “matchy matchy” fashion moments back in the day. “It’s safe to say you and Victoria made what I consider to be a bold choice among couples — of dressing the same,” James said.

“That idea was not my idea,” David replied. When he was then pressed by the presenter on whether he regretted any of the ensembles they wore, he was quick to reply, “most of them.”

David later added, “But they were right at the time. The leather outfits, actually it could have been a lot worse that evening because we were walking out the door and I was like ‘Why don’t we arrive on a motorbike?’ Which obviously back then felt like a good idea, but now talking about it would have been absolutely ridiculous.”

David and Victoria in matching leather outfits. (Photo Credit: Getty Images)

The father-of-four continued, “I’m not saying I regret them because at the time they felt right but now I’m not so sure.”

To mark David’s wedding anniversary, James, David, and the show’s second guest, Emily Blunt, decided to reenact the purple look both David and Victoria wore on their wedding day.

Standing behind a faceless cutout — on which was a photograph of the couple on the day — David, James, and Emily lent their heads to the image with David acting as himself, James as Victoria, and Emily as the couple’s then-baby son Brooklyn.

Hmm… We wonder what Victoria made of it!
