Gwen Stefani and Target Team Up With Harajuku Mini Line

June 10, 2024
blog

Gwen Stefani and son Kingston Rossdale went out to lunch in early October to celebrate Stefani's birthday. The two looked amazing, but it was Kingston who stole the show!

Kingston was seen sporting a unique pair of plaid pants. His clothes were an instant hit, but no one could find them in stores. We now know why!

The plaid pants were a piece from Stefani's Harajuku Mini Line with Target. The collection is made up of apparel and accessories for toddlers, infants and tweens. The prices will range from $4-$60. Stefani's line is set to launch November 13.
