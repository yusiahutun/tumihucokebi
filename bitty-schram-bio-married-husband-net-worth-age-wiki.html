Bitty Schram’s Bio

The widely known actress, Bitty Schram, was born Elizabeth Natalie Schram on 17th July 1968, in Mountainside, New Jersey under the birth sign Cancer. Schram is American by nationality and belongs to White ethnicity.

Regarding her academics, Schram attended the University of Maryland on a tennis scholarship and later earned a degree in advertising design. Having an interest in acting from a very early age, she joined Broadways theatres in order to pursue a career in acting.

Schram made her Hollywood debut in 1992 as Terry in the crime drama film, Fathers & Sons starring Jeff Goldblum, Rory Cochrane, and Rosanna Arquette. The same year, she worked in another movie, A League of Their Own. After working in several movies, she made her TV debut in 1995 in a TV film, Episode: Long Island Fever. Her other movie and tv series credits include Ghost Whisperer, You’ve Got a Friend, Ghost Whisperer, You’ve Got a Friend, Kissing a Fool and more.
