I Just Found a $46 Lookalike for Taylor Swifts $2,900 Designer Coat

July 26, 2024
blog

Taylor Swift has been giving us all of the coat inspo as of late. First, she wore a black-and-white checkered coat on one of her first dates with Chiefs player Travis Kelce. Then, she donned a cherry-red teddy jacket to her beau’s football game and has now followed it up with an even chicer version.

Stella McCartney Tweed Belted Long Coat

This week, Swift wore a polished tweed double-breasted coat from Stella McCartney alongside a coordinating wool-and-tweed mini skirt. The upwards of $2,900 top layer — which comes with a belt that Swift opted to forgo — included a mirage of colors, dramatic notched lapels, buttoned cuffs, balloon sleeves, and a refined, ankle-grazing length. Lucky for you, the original is still available to shop on the Stella McCartney and Farfetch websites. Though, you can achieve a very similar look without dropping nearly $3,000. The secret is in the long hemline, warm hues, and delicate crosshatch pattern — and Nordstrom, Amazon, and more retailers have mastered the Swift-worn look.  

Steve Madden Apparel Prince Coat

This Steve Madden Prince Coat, which is on sale for $100 at Nordstrom, makes the pop star’s cold-weather getup easy to achieve. It comes in neutral shades resembling the original separate, while a 45-inch length and four-button design mesh together with dramatic notched lapels, front pockets, and belted cuffs to give you an almost spot-on lookalike. Throw on a pair of Swift’s exact Sheertex diamond tights with black pumps, and no one will know who’s who.

Tanming Notched Lapel Trench Coat

How can this almost identical trench coat cost $50? I’m just as stumped as you, but nonetheless, I’m grateful for a bargain top layer. This elegant double-breasted coat by Tanming has racked up 1,850 five-star ratings, while offering a delicate plaid design, straight silhouette, discreet pockets, and an attached back belt. Secure this affordable option, and you’ll be twinning with Swift in no time.    

Avec Les Filles Plaid Belted Trench Coat

Avec Les Filles takes us home with its plaid trench coat. Remove the belt for a more Swift-approved look, and enjoy a relaxed fit, soft wool, wide lapels, and a hint of blue color variation, which can similarly be seen in Swift’s original coat if you look closely. Even more exciting, this lookalike is currently 55 percent off, knocking $99 off of the original ticket price.
