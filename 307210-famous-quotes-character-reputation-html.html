Famous quotes on character and reputation

April 20, 2024
blog

Character and reputation are the most important assets that define who we are. They are far more important when compared to a good job, house, car, or even money. Famous scholars, politicians, and psychologists have developed famous quotes on character and reputation to inspire people to work hard to develop the strength of character that is needed to survive in the world today.

The question that most people ask is whether character is more important when compared to reputation. You should never fall into the trap of thinking that reputation is the single sole factor that defines your success. Character plays a critical role in your success when compared to your reputation. It defines who you really are regardless of the circumstances that are surrounding you. Reputation is nothing more than what the other people are thinking about you. This means that every person should be more concerned about their character than they are about their reputation. Here are some of the famous quotes that address the subject of character and reputation that everyone should know.

Famous quotes about character

Here are some famous quotes that focus on the subject of character

  • The best index to the character of a person is how he treats those people that cannot do him any good, and how he treats the people that cannot be able to fight back – Abigail Van Buren.
  • Character builds slowly, but it can be torn down with incredible swiftness– Faith Baldwin
  • The character of an honest man is the most enviable of all titles – George Washington
  • An honorable man may have lots of properties, but none of them is as highly priced as character – Henry Clay
  • Character is doing the right thing even when no one is observing you – J.C. Watts
  • Parents can only give good advice and set you on the right path, but the formation of character lies in the person’s hands – Anne Frank
  • The character of a person is defined by the actions that they take – Catherine Pulsifer
  • Never does a man portray his character more vividly than when proclaiming the character of another person – Winston Churchill
  • It is better to be a man of character than to be a man of means – an Irish proverb
  • Your character is much more important than your looks – Scott Sanford
  • Positive character traits are something that every person can and should develop – Barbara Lewis
  • The character of a person is formed by those challenges that he/she faces and overcomes – Nick Vujicic
  • Nearly every man can stand adversity, but if you want to test the character of man, give him power – Abraham Lincoln
  • Character is one of the qualities that define a human being and propels him/her, on occasion, to greatness – R.J. Palcio
  • The true character of a man comes out when he is drunk – Charlie Chaplin
  • Knowledge will give you power, but character gives you respect – Bruce Lee
