Full Moon in Cancer Will Affect Each Zodiac Sign 2023

June 15, 2024
blog

The last full moon of 2023 has arrived, and it’s no secret that emotions are running high. This is common when considering the time of year, but if you’re wondering how the full moon in Cancer will affect your zodiac sign, embrace your feelings fully. Whether in the past or present, your emotional experiences tell a story that’ll continue to shape your individual reality. The truth isn’t always pretty, so don’t be afraid to acknowledge what’s being illuminated to you at this time. More importantly, always remember that your feelings are valid.

Governed by the ever-changing moon, Cancer is symbolic of the divine mother archetype. It can be as nurturing as it is destructive, similar to a mother’s womb and how it has the power to hold the infinite cycle of death and rebirth at its core. This cardinal water sign encourages you to look within and discover what truly nourishes you from the inside out. After all, in addition to representing themes surrounding the home and family, Cancer can speak about the way you mother yourself when your own mother isn’t around to take care of you. The maternal side of your family holds the key to this water sign’s sorcery, as it carries a sea of ancestral wisdom, matriarchal magic, and kitchen witchery for herbal remedies.

Heightened intuition is inevitable during this lunar phase, but what is illuminated cannot be denied when the moon peaks in its sign of rulership, which also happens to be Cancer. Psychically overwhelming and cloaked in heavy nostalgia, this year’s full moon in Cancer will adorn the night sky on December 26 at 7:33 p.m. ET and via 4 degrees of this cardinal water sign. It’s no coincidence that this lunation is happening during the holiday season, as we are called to take a closer look at our family dynamics and emotional patterns. With the support of a harmonious sextile to Jupiter—Greater Benefic, and the planet of abundance, expansion, and spiritual wisdom—we are simultaneously reminded of the safety within our heart space. This could also be a celebration of your emotional journey thus far.

Whether it be an influx of emotions that feel like ocean waves above your head or a newfound perspective on past experiences you’ve deeply repressed, it’s time to unleash the floodgates. Jupiter will harmonize with the sun in Capricorn, highlighting everything from your personal values and traditions, which may continue to validate your emotions. Saturn is also a key player, as it sextiles Jupiter and forms a steady trine with the moon. This is about taking accountability for your actions, feelings, and perhaps the pain stemming from your past. There is also strong paternal energy, especially when considering the influence of Capricorn season and Saturn—its planetary ruler—so you could be wrapping up a previous cycle surrounding your family structure or unit.

On the dark side, Mercury retrograde will be in close proximity with aggressive Mars in Sagittarius. Despite the healing frequency stemming from this lunation, we are also prone to hot-headed outbursts and brutally honest conversations that may hurt people’s feelings in the process. Be discerning with your delivery, and think twice before you start a wildfire with your words.
