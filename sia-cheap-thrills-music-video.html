Watch Sia's &quot;Cheap Thrills&quot; Music Video

May 22, 2024
blog

Sia just premiered the music video for "Cheap Thrills," the latest single off her album This Is Acting, which dropped in January.

The video focuses on one scene starring three dancers in nude leotards featured on a backlit stage. One of them is Dance Moms prodigy Maddie Ziegler, who's previously made haunting appearances in Sia's videos for "Chandelier," "Elastic Heart," and "Big Girls Cry." 

Meanwhile the singer herself remains in the corner in her two-tone wig disguise as she lets her dancers take the spotlight throughout the video with energized, whimsical choreography.
