Early life

Jordan Emanuel is an African American lady born on 17 April 1992 in Baltimore, Maryland, U.S., but grew up in Basking Ridge, New Jersey. Her late mother, Paula Birdsong Emanuel, was a biomedical engineer who studied at Howard and Johns Hopkins University.

Her father, Lawrence David Emanuel, is of African heritage. The personality has not been revealed in public domains whether or not she has siblings.

According to her LinkedIn profile, Jordan started schooling at Far Hills Country Day School and later graduated from The Hun School of Princeton. After high school, she enrolled at the University of Miami, majoring in Music Business, Broadcast Journalism, and Art History.

The personality completed her studies and earned the degree in 2014. During her college days, Jordan also took a short course in Radio DJ at the University of Dayton in 2011.

Jordan's diverse background in education prepared her well as she began her career journey working as a production assistant intern and a radio DJ. She developed the No Boundaries show on Flyer Radio. These early roles greatly contributed to her deep understanding of the media industry.
