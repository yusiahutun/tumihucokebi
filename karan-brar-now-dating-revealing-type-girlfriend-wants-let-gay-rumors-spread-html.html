We must agree that on-screen youngster characters these days are no less than any other experienced actors in Hollywood. However, today's contender is Karan Brar, famous as Chirag Gupta from the 'Diary of a Wimpy Kid.'

With his growing fame, the 'Jessie' actor's fans are more interested in his dating life and ask related questions. Does he have a girlfriend? Is he single? Is he gay? 

If you are one of his fans, keep scrolling to find your answers.

Is Karan Brar Single or Has a Girlfriend?

Karan Brar's fans can breathe a sigh of relief as he is not gay. However, it seems like the actor is single. 

Yet many sources claim that he has a girlfriend. He is said to be dating Gamer's Guide to Pretty Much Everything's actress, Sophie Reynolds.

Karan with his supposed girlfriend, Sophie Reynolds. (Photo: Instagram)

However, neither Karan nor Sophie has said anything related to their rumors. Looking at their Instagram, they do spend a lot of time together, but their posts don't hint at them being in a romantic relationship.

Karan, who has been asked about his love life for a long time, took his official Instagram account to answer those questions.

In April 2015, Karan shared a video on his Instagram where he revealed being single. He also stated the qualities that he looks for in his girlfriend-to-be. He wrote-

“Right now, I'm not dating anyone but the kind of girl that I like is the one that makes me laugh. I have a lot of humor in me and I love to tell jokes so I would love a girl that can do the same.”

Even after more than seven years after revealing the qualities that Karan seeks in his girlfriend, he is single. He still hasn't found his humorous girl who could make him laugh.

And Karan not having a girlfriend has sparked gay rumors. But, he has been private when it comes to dating and affairs.

Even if he rarely speaks about his dating affairs, he often talks about his relationship with his family.

Karan Brar's Supportive Parents

Though Karan is miles away when it comes to dating and affairs, he is very active in talking about his relationship with his family.

On the occasion of Father's day, he shared an image of himself with his father, Harinder Brar, and wished him Happy Father's Day.

Karan wishes his father on Father's Day. (Photo: Instagram)

Karan also wrote a letter to his father, revealing how blessed he is to be his son. He explained how amazing his father is, who gave up everything for the better future of his kids.

Karan Brar's letter to his Dad Harinder Brar for Father's Day. (Photo: Justjared)

Likewise, Karan has also been flaunting his relationship with his mother, Jasbinder, on his Instagram. In May 2017, he shared an image of his mother, Jasbinder Brar, with a caption-

“Happy Mother's Day to the greatest mom a kid could ask for. Words cannot describe how much I love you mom :)”

Karan wishes his mother on Mother's Day. (Photo: Instagram)

Talking about Karan's siblings, he has an older sister named Sabreena Brar.
