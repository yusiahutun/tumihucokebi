Meaning of Cuando Perriabas by Bad Bunny

April 08, 2024
blog

In Bad Bunny's song "Cuando Perriabas," the overall theme revolves around embracing one's sexual desires and indulging in pleasure. The lyrics of the song convey a sense of liberation and freedom, urging the listeners to let go of societal constraints and fully explore their sensuality. Each standout lyric helps to develop different themes that contribute to the song's overall message.One of the standout lyrics in the song is, "A tu novio de novela, lo pongo en el logo de los Bulls" (I put your romantic boyfriend on the Bulls' logo). This lyric highlights the theme of breaking societal norms and expectations. It suggests that the protagonist doesn't follow conventional relationship ideals and instead finds pleasure in unconventional ways. The lyric embodies a rebellious and fearless attitude towards love and relationships, challenging the traditional narrative.Another important lyric in the song is, "La Barbie que no venden en la tienda, la dura del perreo, es una leyenda" (The Barbie they don't sell in the store, the perreo queen, is a legend). This lyric emphasizes the empowerment and strength of the female character. It portrays her as a legendary figure who defies stereotypes and embraces her sexuality with confidence. The theme here centers around female empowerment and breaking free from societal expectations of how women should behave.Additionally, the lyric "Por la forma en que perriabas, baby, no te hagas, que yo sé que le metías hasta abajo" (By the way you used to twerk, baby, don't pretend, I know you went all the way down) delves into the theme of self-expression and authenticity. It celebrates the uninhibited way of dancing known as "perriar" or twerking. Through this lyric, Bad Bunny encourages individuals to embrace their true selves and express their desires without shame or inhibition.Furthermore, the song includes an interlude featuring Plan B that introduces the theme of reggaeton culture and its unapologetic, explicit lyrics. This theme explores the history and evolution of the genre, its impact on society, and its resilience in the face of criticism. It highlights how reggaeton has become an outlet for self-expression, allowing artists and listeners to explore their desires and indulge in pleasure.In conclusion, "Cuando Perriabas" encompasses several powerful themes that contribute to its overall message of sexual liberation and self-expression. Through standout lyrics that touch on breaking norms, female empowerment, authenticity, and celebrating reggaeton culture, Bad Bunny encourages listeners to embrace their desires, let go of societal constraints, and find freedom in pleasure. The song serves as an anthem for those seeking to break free from societal expectations and live life on their own terms.
