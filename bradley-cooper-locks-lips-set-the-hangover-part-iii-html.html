Bradley Cooper has earned something of a lothario reputation after  being  linked to a host of Hollywood actresses, including Scarlett Johansson, Jennifer Aniston and Denise Richards.

So it should be no surprise to see another woman falling for his charms after he was spotted locking lips during a public embrace.

Cooper was also seen receiving direction from filmmaker Todd Philips as they filmed the scene.

Cooper, Helms and Zach Galifianakis are reprising their wild boy roles, with a host of other actors returning from the series, including Justin Bartha,  Heather Graham, Mike Tyson and Ken Jeong.

John Goodman is a newcomer to the cast.

Cooper receives direction from filmmaker Todd Phillips

Graham, who played the stripper Jade in the first movie, can't wait to star in the third instalment after she missed out on the first sequel.

However, she told E! Online that the producers are so scared the storyline will be leaked they've gone to extraordinary lengths to keep it under wraps.

She said: 'They've been very weird. I don't think they want me to tell anyone anything. First, they just gave me my scenes. It's like the CIA. It's not like we're going to send the script to millions of people. Why would we?

The films follow the post-drunken misadventures of The Wolfpack - played by Zach Galifianakis, Cooper and Helms

'It took them like a few weeks to send me the script and it's got my name on every page/ It's as if it's a top secret mission to save the world."

However, last year Zach appeared to have let slip a potential storyline to Rolling Stone, claiming the movie will focus on his character escaping from a mental home with help from the Wolf Pack.

Series director Todd Philips said about the movie in March: 'I'm so excited to embark on another Hangover film with Bradley, Ed and Zach. We're going to surprise a lot of people with the final chapter we have planned.

'It will be a fitting conclusion to our three-part opera of mayhem, despair and bad decisions.'
