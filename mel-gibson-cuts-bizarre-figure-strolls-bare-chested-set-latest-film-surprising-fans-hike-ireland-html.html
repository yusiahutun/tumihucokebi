Fans Heather Fogarty and her pals Michael O’Connor and Shane Gallagher bumped into the star during their hike in Ireland.  

'Climbed the sugar loaf today,' Heather wrote, alongside the snapshot on Instagram. 'And to top it all off, we met Mel Gibson at the summit.'
