Jennifer Lopez Wore the Sexiest Version of This Wardrobe Basic

May 07, 2024
blog

Jennifer Lopez isn’t exactly known for her subdued fashion; this year alone, she’s draped herself in flowers on at least three occasions. And in that now-famous Dunkin’ Super Bowl commercial, she kept things true to form with a pair of seriously distressed jeans, a matching denim jacket, sparkly pointed-toe boots, and packed-on statement jewelry. But underneath, things were actually quite basic — though, in true J.Lo-fashion, still remarkably sexy.

Lopez posted an Instagram story following the ad, revealing a simple white tank top was beneath her all-denim look. The tank featured a high neckline, ribbed design, and a seriously cropped hem, making it perhaps the sexiest version of the wardrobe basic I’ve ever seen. 

Immediately, I imagined wearing the same top with classic jeans or even a pair of high-waisted, wide-leg trousers, giving the dressed-up style a laid-back, cool-girl feel. So I took to Amazon to find affordable alternatives and discovered seven lookalike cropped tanks with prices starting at $7 apiece.

Shop 7 Cropped White Tank Tops

Meladyan Racerback Ribbed Tank Top

Meladyan’s ribbed tank top is a dead ringer for Lopez’s with the same high neckline, cropped hem, and form-fitting design — though, according to fans, “It’s not too cropped…it’s not too thin, and not too tight.” “The cut is so flattering…really cute, and feminine,” said one shopper who deemed the top the “best staple.” And per someone else, the top goes with everything. “It looks great with high-waisted pants and skirts,” they wrote, adding that the tank top is so thick that even the white style isn’t see-through.

Laslulu Cropped Tank Top

While Lopez opted for a more fitted style, Laslulu’s tank top is perfect for anyone wanting a looser fit. This style still has the same key details — from the neck to the ribbing — but flows out rather than cutting in at the waist. According to one person, the top “fits in all the right places and is loose enough to be comfortable without being flowy.” “I love the weight of it and the way the material drapes. It's very flattering,” said someone else, while a final customer gushed about the cut, describing it as being “super flattering,” “not too revealing,” and perfect for high-waisted pants.

Hanes Originals 3-Pack Racerback Cropped Tank Top 

With Hanes’ three-pack of tank tops, you can get Lopez’s look for just $7 apiece. This cotton-blend top features a built-in bra that left even one shopper with a 36DDD-sized chest “very satisfied.” According to one person who described the tank top as “so comfortable and supportive,” the style “fits great” and “stays in place.” What’s more, the multipack is available in three colorways: one featuring only white tanks, another, all black, and a third that comes with white, black, and gray tanks (which is already in my cart).

Lopez’s cropped white tank top is a sexy take on the wardrobe basic my closet is missing. Shop more Amazon styles inspired by her look, below.
