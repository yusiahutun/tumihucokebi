Teachers Service Commission contacts, head office and branches

September 25, 2024
blog

The Teachers Service Commission (TSC) is an independent commission formed under the Kenyan constitution to manage the human resources in the education sector. In layman’s language, this is the body behind employment and management of teachers. Are you in search of the Teachers Service Commission contacts? Read on to get more than you expected.

The TSC body that is operating currently has a very interesting history. The formation of TSC dates back to 1957. Kenya had barely acquired its independence before this commission was formed. At that time, the British found it essential to have teachers’ matters handled by a decentralized system. Currently, the TSC is formed under Article 237(1) of the Kenyan constitution we voted for in the year 2010. So, do you have any idea on what the TSC Kenya contacts today are? How about the Teachers Service Commission telephone contacts? The Teachers Service Commission Kenya contacts are as follows:

Services offered by the Teachers Service Commission (TSC)

TSC offers a wide range of services relating to the education ministry. Some of the most common services the body offers include registration of trained teachers, assigning employed teachers for services in different public schools or institutions, transfer and promotion of teachers, discipline among the employed teachers, employing registered teachers and terminating the employment of teachers in case of failure to follow certain guidelines.

For you to get any services offered by TSC, you don’t necessarily need to travel over long distances to their head office. Grab the necessary Teachers Service Commission contacts, be it the email, location or their postal addresses and get served quickly. Remember! Services are offered only in between government working hours. Good luck.
