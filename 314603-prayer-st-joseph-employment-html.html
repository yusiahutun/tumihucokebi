Do you find yourself in need of prayer looking for work these days? Prayers are a direct way to speak to God and have him answer to our needs. In the Catholic church, there is a prayer to get a job you applied for. Most people in the Catholic church say a prayer to St. Joseph for employment since he is the patron of the universal church.

Saint Joseph supplication for employment is a novena for employment miracles. Prayer to St. Joseph for employment is encouraged to those seeking a job. St Joseph was the father of Jesus, and he is related to work since he was a carpenter. Besides, he is divinely an intercessor for work-related issues to the patron saint of job interviews. You can thus ask St. Joseph to intercede on your behalf via the novena prayers for employment.
