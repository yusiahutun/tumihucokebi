How Old Is Rapper YFN Lucci, and What Is His Net Worth?

September 09, 2024
blog

Atlanta-based rapper YFN Lucci first rose to fame in 2017 with his hit single, “Everyday We Lit.” He then got into a high profile and heavily criticized relationship with Lil Wayne’s daughter, Reginae Carter, around 2018. While it’s unclear if the on-and-off-again couple is currently dating again, she’s seemingly sticking by him as he’s in jail on racketeering charges. How old is Lucci, and what is his net worth?

How old is YFN Lucci?

Born Feb. 16, 1991, Rayshawn Lamar Bennett is currently 30-years-old. After taking music seriously for a few years, the Atlanta-based rapper signed with Think It’s A Game Entertainment in 2014.

He also dropped his debut mixtape Wish Me Well the same year, followed by the sequel, which hit the 183rd spot on the US Billboard 200 chart two years later.

Throughout 2016, he received media attention as XXL and Vibe magazine featured a remix to the hit single from his second mixtape, Key to the Streets, in their best songs of the year lists.

In 2017, his debut extended play, Long Live Nut, featured single, “Everyday We Lit,” which turned out to be one of his highest-charting, hitting number 33 on the Hot 100 chart. He has since released his first studio album, Ray Ray from Summerhill in 2018 and Wish Me Well 3 in 2020.

He previously dated Lil Wayne’s daughter, Reginae Carter

Around Feb. 2018, Lucci and rapper Lil Wayne’s daughter, Reginae Carter, sparked dating rumors, and they later confirmed their relationship. However, many fans, including her parents, didn’t support the union due to the age gap, as she was around 19 and he was about 27 when they began dating.

Additionally, the rapper has a few kids by different women. The couple reportedly split in Aug. 2019 due to his participation in the NSFW Cucumber Challenge. She later confirmed their breakup during an episode of the VH1 family reality series, T.I. & Tiny: The Family Hustle.

However, the two seemed to stay in contact and reconnected in Oct. 2020 when they reportedly vacationed together. They revealed they were back together in Dec. 2020, only a month before his arrest.

She acknowledged the situation and released a statement implying they had spoken, noting, “Ray wants you all to know he’s in good spirits and doing well,” and “he’ll be home soon.” Even though her comment seems supportive of Lucci, their current relationship status is unclear. 

What is YFN Lucci’s net worth?

In Jan. 2021, Atlanta authorities issued a warrant for the rapper’s arrest on charges relating to a shooting where one person was injured, and another was killed.

After being released on a $500,000 bond, he, and 11 others, turned themselves in on racketeering charges due to their alleged involvement in gang activity.

Lucci’s lawyer has stated his client is not guilty nor a gang member. Outside of music, the rapper and “B.E.D.” singer Jacquees were featured in two campaigns for fashion lifestyle company Sean John.

According to Celebrity Net Worths, Lucci has an estimated net worth of $500,000.
