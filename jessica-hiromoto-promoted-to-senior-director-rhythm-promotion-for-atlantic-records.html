Jessica Hiromoto Promoted to Senior Director, Rhythm Promotion For Atlantic Records

July 29, 2024
blog

Jessica Hiromoto promoted to Senior Director, Rhythm Promotion for Atlantic Records. Courtesy Image.

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) – Jessica Hiromoto has been promoted to Senior Director, Rhythm Promotion for Atlantic Records.

Hiromoto previously served as West Coast Regional Promotion Manager.

Of the appointment, Andrea Ganis, President, Promotion for Atlantic Records, said: “We are thrilled to announce that Jessica Hiromoto has been promoted to Senior Director, Rhythm Promotion, reporting to SVP Pop and Rhythm Promotion John McMann. Jessica (aka Mo) has been at Atlantic as our Los Angeles regional for the last six years and her knowledge of the format, diligence, overall smarts, and work ethic make her the perfect executive for this position.”

McMann added: “Mo’s wonderful spirit and force of nature tenacity has shone through from day one. She is a tremendous complement to the Atlantic Rhythmic team, and we look forward to her continuing to help drive the culture and break new artists in the years ahead.”

Randy “DJ RaiZe” Reyes was also promoted to Director, Rhythmic & National Director, Mixshow. Reyes previously served as National Director of Pop/Rhythm Mixshow.
