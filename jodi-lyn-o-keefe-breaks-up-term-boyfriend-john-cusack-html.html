After a six years-long relationship, Jodi Lyn O’Keefe and John Cusack broke up. They were in a relationship since 2003 and kept the romantic journey till 2009.

Sadly they are no longer a couple anymore. It is heard that the pair broke up over a disagreement about whether to get married or not.

Jodi and John Break Up

After being in a relationship for a long time, it’s natural for couples to think about their future. Jodi did the same. She was in love with Cusack for six years and expected that their relationship would find a new meaning. Jodi Lyn O'Keefe wanted to be married to her boyfriend and settled down.

Jodi Lyn O'Keefe sharing coffee with her ex-boyfriend John Cusack (2004) (Photo Credit: healthyceleb.com)

But things didn't go as planned. What she expected from John couldn’t be fulfilled. John couldn’t support her for this next step in their relationship. John didn’t seem ready to take the big leap and didn’t seem to be in the mood for marriage.

When they were dating, she hoped that he would change his mind about marriage, but when he couldn’t do so, she finally decided to call it quits for good. 

Before the split, the two also took a Florida beach vacation to try if things would work out for them. But a witness says that once they returned from their vacation, “they were freezing to each other, bickering.” So, the vacation didn’t prove to be a success for both of them.

Previously, she dated Don Johnson in 1997 and also had a relationship with Jaime Gonzales in the year 1998.

Is Jodi Lyn O'Keefe Married?

After she broke up with John Cusack, it seemed like her trust in relationships had blown away.

But it looks like we were wrong about that. As per her Instagram post, Jodi Lyn O'Keefe has found a partner for herself. She is in a relationship with a man named Douglas Little.

The big news came to light when both of them shared the same picture on Valentine's Day on their respective Instagram handle.

While Jodi Lyn's caption read, Happy Love Day, Douglas, on his post, wrote: The key to my lock and the butter to my bread. Happy Love Day.

Going through her Instagram, it suggested that they are together since 2016 as she had shared a post with him in late 2016.

On the other side, Douglas also shares pictures on his Insta handle and doesn't shy away from flaunting his love for her.   

For the time being, the couple is enjoying each other's company as boyfriend and girlfriend. And hopefully, in the near future, they would step up their relationship and get married.

Jodi Lyn O’ Keefe's Bio

Jodi was born on 10 October, in Cliffwood Beach, New Jersey, to Nareen and Jack O’Keefe. She is an American actress and model who, at the age of 17, portrayed Cassidy Bridges's role on Nash Bridges.

She is known for her role as Gretchen Morgan in “Prison Break” and as Josette “Jo” Laughlin in the “Vampire Diaries.” She began her career as a model at the age of eight. Keefe made her big-screen debut in 1998 in Halloween H20: 20 Years Later.

Since then, Jodi has appeared in a number of movies & TV series that have garnered her a lot of popularity and fan base.
