PVRIS Announces North American Tour With Pale Waves And Bruses

July 08, 2024
blog

SANTA ANA, CA (CelebrityAccess) – PVRIS announces its highly-anticipated 2024 North American headlining tour with special guests Pale Waves and Bruses. Produced by Live Nation, the 26-city tour kicks off on June 1 at Observatory OC in Santa Ana, CA, making stops in Salt Lake City, Chicago, New York, Toronto, and more before concluding at The Wiltern in Los Angeles on July 12.

This marks PVRIS’ first headlining North American run since the release of her critically acclaimed fourth studio album, Evergreen, last July, which earned her the first No.1 on the UK Official Rock and Metal Albums Chart of her career.

Pale Waves, formed in Manchester in 2014, is often described as pop-punk. They released their debut EP, All the Things I Never Said, in 2018, followed by their first studio album, My Mind Makes Noises the same year. The band’s sophomore effort Who Am I? peaked at No. 3 on the UK Album Chart and hit No. 1 on the UK Indie Albums chart. The band consists of Heather Baron-Gracie (vocals, guitar), Charlie Wood (bass), Hugo Silvani (guitar), and Ciara Doran (drums).

Citi is the official card of the tour. Citi card member’s presale began Monday (January 8). It will run until Thursday (January 11) at 10 pm, with additional presales throughout the week ahead of the general on-sale beginning Friday (January 12) at 10 am local time via Live Nation.
