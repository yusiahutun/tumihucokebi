Businessman Jamal Rohosafi has advised men not to feel like fools whenever they marry women who sired kids with other men.

Jamal: It's not foolishness to marry single mums

Jamal gave his two cents on men who married ladies with kids, and the children's father disappeared.

In a world where a man is shunned for marrying and caring for a lady with kids, Jamal actually encouraged it.

Fool is the biological dad

The father of two told men they were not fools for loving single mothers, and the fool was the kid's biological father.

The businessman termed the biological dad a fool since he ran away from his responsibility of caring for his children.

He said:

"Marrying a Lady with kids Doesn't mean you're a Fool. A Fool is the Biological Dad who Ran away from His Responsibilities."

Jamal had previously taken socialite Amber Ray and flaunted her on social media whilst spoiling her with exotic vacations. Amber is a mother of one.

He, however, had a wife, Amira, who got publicly humiliated online, but after cutting ties with Amber, he recently asked for her forgiveness.

Amira, who revealed the apology had taken her to one of the darkest times in her life as so much disrespect happened but asked God to give her grace as some wounds do not just heal.

Ogana says single mums make best spouses

In another story, TUKO.co.ke reported that Cindy Ogana a panellist on The Trend, shared that single mums were not born out of choice.

She revealed she was a single mum before getting married and understood the troubles of single parenthood.

Ogana noted that 40% of households consisted of mostly single parents in Kenya, and they made the best spouses, so people shouldn't be shy in choosing them.
