Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift have found one key element that can make their relationship last a lifetime.

Kansas City Chiefs tight end Travis Kelce has found himself at the center of attention after sparking dating rumors with Taylor Swift. After a few months of getting to know each other, the celebrity couple are moving on to epic milestones as sources claim, they are “so in love.”

Amid some claims that the pair is moving too soon, enough psychological clues expose the green flags in their relationship which might hold the key to their everlasting happiness.

Travis Kelce in it for the long haul according to psychology

It is not easy to date one of the most globally popular superstars who is considered not just a music legend with twelve Grammys, but also a pop cultural icon whose songs become anthems for millions. But Travis Kelce is not complaining. If anything, his family claims, he seems happier than ever.

Speaking of his heart-fluttering romance with Tay Tay, Travis can’t help but gush over her qualities that made him fall in love. He talks about one attribute of hers that many won’t know, revealing that she is “hilarious,” and “a genius,” at the same time.

In psychology, laughing is a key factor in long-term relationships. Studies have found that couples who can appreciate each other’s humor and laugh at their jokes automatically unlock a key to their long-term happiness.

Taylor and Travis are like two “peas in a pod”

Friends close to Taylor and Travis have called them two “peas in a pod” and Travis agrees as he shares how their compatibility is like no other. Explaining the dynamics between the two, Travis reveals: “Everybody knows I’m a family guy. Her team is her family. Her family does a lot of stuff in terms of the tour, the marketing, being around, so I think she has a lot of those values as well, which is right up my alley.”

It’s not just the compatibility that makes Travis and Taylor a ‘couple goal’ who are already making their holiday plans together, the 34-year-old sports celebrity shares that he is also ready to accept what comes while dating a star of her ‘aura.’

Travis is serious about her and it shows as he says: “Obviously I’ve never dated anyone with that kind of aura about them…. I’ve never dealt with it.

“But at the same time, I’m not running away from any of it…. The scrutiny she gets, how much she has a magnifying glass on her, every single day, paparazzi outside her house, outside every restaurant she goes to, after every flight she gets off, and she’s just living, enjoying life. When she acts like that I better not be the one acting all strange.”
