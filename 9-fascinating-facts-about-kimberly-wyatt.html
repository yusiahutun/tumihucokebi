The Pussycat Dolls were formed in 1995

Kimberly Wyatt rose to fame as one of the members of the popular girl group, The Pussycat Dolls. The group was originally formed in 1995 as a burlesque dance troupe before transitioning into a music group.

Kimberly Wyatt is a trained dancer

Before pursuing a career in music, Kimberly Wyatt honed her skills as a professional dancer. She studied ballet, tap, jazz, and contemporary dance, and even trained at the prestigious Joffrey Ballet School in New York City.

She appeared on the reality show “Got To Dance”

In 2009, Kimberly Wyatt became a judge on the British reality show “Got To Dance.” Her expertise in dance and charismatic personality made her a fan favorite and propelled her further into the spotlight.

Kimberly Wyatt has appeared in various TV shows

Aside from her music and dance career, Kimberly Wyatt has also made appearances on several television shows. She competed on the reality show “Celebrity MasterChef” and has been a guest judge on “Dancing on Ice.”

In 2010, Kimberly Wyatt released her debut solo album titled “Breath On Me.” The album showcased her unique musical style and received positive reviews from critics.

Wyatt won the reality show “Celebrity MasterChef”

She is a judge on “Dance Dance Dance”

Since 2017, Kimberly Wyatt has been a judge on the ITV reality show “Dance Dance Dance.” Her expertise and passion for dance make her a valuable addition to the judging panel.
