Sarah Silverman pays tribute after motherBeth Ann dies

August 29, 2024
blog

Sarah Silverman paid an emotional tribute to her mother on Monday when she revealed she had passed away.

Beth Ann O'Hara, who was divorced the comedy star's father Donald Silverman, died last Wednesday at the age of 73.

The Million Ways To Die in the West star said her mother had taught her many lessons about life in a message she posted on Twitter.

Passed away: Sarah Silverman revealed on Monday her mother Beth Ann O'Hara had died last week

The 44-year-old said: 'She was a know-it-all in overalls and two different coloured socks. And defiantly no bra.

'The woman who, when I was little, gave me detailed direction to a restaurant ladies room then secretly watched as I found my way to it on my own.

'The woman who went back to college at 42 and skinny dipped in ponds, and told me never to protect myself from a broken heart because it was so very worth the pain to get to experience love.'

Passionate about the theatre, Beth had founded the New Thalian Players in New Hampshire and produced and directed 50 plays over quarter of a century.

Tribute: The emotional comedienne said her mother told her 'never to protect myself from a broken heart'

According to NHPR, she was known as 'mom' to many aspiring actors she had worked with.

While Sarah is her most famous child, she was the mother to three other daughters, and had remarried after divorcing Sarah's father.

The proud comedienne had presented her mother with a lifetime achievement award at the New Hampshire Theater Awards in 2003.
