Carol Vorderman, 57, leaves jaws on the floor in naked illusion gown

September 13, 2024

The former Countdown presenter has had a very hectic schedule of late.

Carol has had a regular spot on morning programme Lorraine, giving dating advice to the over 50s.

And she took on the role of host once more tonight, as she got behind the helm of the Pride of Britain Awards 2018.

As ever, Carol looked sensational as she showed off her ageless beauty in a figure-flashing gown.

Carol left onlookers amazed, as she rocked a lacy navy frock, which showed off her fantastic figure.

The dress, based a naked illusion effect, as the nude fabric under the neath, made it appear that Carol was completely nude.

Adding to her glamorous get-up, Carol styled her brunette locks into big bouncy curls and added a sultry smoky eye shadow to her big browns.

Carol has been delighting fans with her honest and upfront approach to dating on Lorraine, however she admitted she felt some trepidation about doing the miniseries.

Speaking to the Daily Express, Carol said: “This really seemed to have struck a nerve.

“When the Editor asked me to do the mini series, I said ‘I won’t do it if it’s about ‘wear this dress and everything in your life will be perfect,’ or in the spirit of ‘everyone is looking for someone’ because the truth is women in their 50s aren’t like that at all.”

She added: “Luckily, that’s not what the programme was looking for.

“I’ve had so many people coming up to me, in the hairdressers, walking in the street, on the train saying seeing the mini series honestly changed their attitudes and sometimes the way they now view their lives!”
