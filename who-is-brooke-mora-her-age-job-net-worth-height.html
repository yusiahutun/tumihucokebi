Hulu’s new original series, Back in the Groove, features three single women, all in their 40s, stuck in the grind of their everyday lives. As per the show’s synopsis, they will get out of their comfort zones and check into The Groove Hotel, a magical resort on the beautiful island of the Dominican Republic, where they will rediscover youth once again, live joyously, and hopefully fall in love with young men. Brooke Mora is one of the three lucky ladies starring in the Hulu original.

Let’s learn everything about her in the wiki article below.

Brooke Mora On Back In The Groove

Hosted by Taye Diggs, who helped Stella get her groove back, Back In The Groove wants the cast of the show to connect with their youthful side once again.

Besides Brooke Mora, the other stars of the show are Sparkle Hyche, who is a 43-year-old Atlanta-based influencer, and Steph Michaels, a 41-year-old Miami native.

Brooke first announced her participation in Back In The Groove via an Instagram post on November 4, 2022. She shared a promotional video of the show and wrote, “I’m excited to announce the release of #BackInTheGroove! @stephmichaels, @sparklethestylist, and I packed our bags and checked into The Groove Hotel!! ”

A week ahead of the show’s premiere, which is happening on December 5th, Brooke wrote, “Hi my name is Brooke and I want it all!,” on her Instagram.

On November 30th, Brooke explained that the guys will be fighting over her and her groove sisters, Steph Michaels and Sparkle Hyche, in the show through Instagram.

Brooke Mora Job

Brooke Mora is a personal trainer by profession. She founded Brooke Mora Training in March 2015 in the Greater Los Angeles area. Since then, she has been coaching her clients to reach their dream physiques.

Recently, Brooke has also started working as a Director of Rental Services at Canyon Luxury Group, LLC according to her LinkedIn handle.

The reality TV star also runs another business called Liquid Collagen.

Brooke’s past job includes Director Of Operations at Global Merch Group, Executive Assistant at Global Med Group, Personal Trainer at Equinox, and DVD Production Coordinator at The Walt Disney Company.

In case you did not know, Brooke is also a former fitness model and pro bodybuilder. As a fitness model, she has appeared on the cover pages of various magazines.

Brooke’s most significant achievement as a pro bodybuilder was 16th place at the inaugural Bikini Olympia in 2010. She qualified for the competition by winning 2nd place in the 2010 IFBB Tournament of Champions. Some of her other achievements include the 2009 NPC Excalibur Bodybuilding and Figure Championships – 5th Place, the 2010 NPC MuscleContest Bodybuilding, Figure & Bikini Championships – 1st Place, and the 2010 NPC USA Championships – 1st Place.

Brooke Mora Family

Coming to Brooke’s family, she has already lost her father. Her mother, Lori Morrow, is however still alive.

Brooke’s brother, Fonz Mora, is married to his wife Hannah Catalina, who has degrees like PA-C, MPH in Surgical Oncology. The couple married in September 2019. Fonz and Hannah have two kids, Greyson Wolfe Mora and Myles Rowan. Greyson was born on March 26, 2016, whereas Myles first opened his eyes on earth on March 17, 2018.

Furthermore, Brooke’s former boyfriend and father of her baby daughter is Dylan Elise. As per his Instagram handle, he is a touring and recording drummer.
