CAPE COD, MASSACHUSETTS: Shanice Henderson is about to stir some drama in 'Summer House: Martha's Vineyard' Episode 6. The reality star will become the center of attraction as she flaunts her curves while skinny dipping in the pool. Will this make the committed women with boyfriends in the house jealous?

Undoubtedly! And, the one who will react to Shanice's provocation is none other than Bria Fleming. The reality star's boyfriend Simon Marco was seen making an entrance in the show in last episode. Now that he has become one of the members of the house, Bria might find him being involved in some unnecessary drama. 

Shanice Henderson goes 'b**t naked'

The preview clip of Episode 6 shows the men and women going their separate ways on their day out. The guys are seen anticipating what the girls have been doing without them. On the other hand, the preview clip shows girls enjoying themselves as they drink and dance. Moreover, the preview clip addresses a drama that is about to be featured in the upcoming episode. Shanice will be seen sparking jealousy in Bria as she skinny dips in the pool. Someone in the preview clip is seen saying, "Get dressed Shanice!" Furthermore, Bria in a confessional video says, "Shanice really? I don't care if you're a Playboy bunny, porn star, or whatever. You don't do that." Later in the clip, Jasmine is seen saying to Silas, "Shanice is b**t naked with Simon in Jacuzzi." This made the cast members look up in shock. Indeed, it is speculated that there will be an altercation between Shanice and Bria.

Will Bria doubt her boyfriend?

She might! But, the preview clip clears that Bria will doubt Shanice more than her boyfriend. There are various options to resolve the drama. Simon could either walk out of the jacuzzi after seeing Shanice go "b**t naked" or he can either show no interest in Shanice. But, at the same time, if Simon shows any interest in Shanice's skinny dipping then Bria will be doubting her boyfriend's commitment to her. However, before this happens, there will be fight between Shanice and Bria.

Shanice is seen saying in the preview clip, "I'm back." Other cast members are seen greeting Shanice. Well, her return is about to bring a lot of drama.
