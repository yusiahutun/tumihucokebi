Jonas Brothers I Believe Song Lyrics Meaning Explained

September 27, 2024
blog

Happiness has officially begun for the Jonas Brothers, who've released their comeback album at midnight.

The first two songs on on the Jonases' 14-track album are pre-released hits. First comes the upbeat "Sucker," which dropped in March, followed by the laid-back California-esque "Cool." Then a couple slots down comes a standout track, "I Believe."

The song, one of the many romantic cuts from Happiness Begins," was a "love letter" from Nick Jonas to his spouse, Priyanka Chopra.

"And this song is a love letter to my wife," he told Apple Music. "I'm so excited to play it on tour."

Joe even added, "We listened to it a lot on your bachelor party. It sounds even better by the beach." Nick's bachelor party took place in early November, which means the song's been done for seven months.

The JoBros composed this song with producer Greg Kurstin, who's worked with Adele, Beck, and other artist the brothers "love and respect." He helped the group "define what the album was going to sound like," Nick revealed.
