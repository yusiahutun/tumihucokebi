What is JTWhatsapp, and is it safe to download? All the details

WhatsApp has always been the number one instant messaging platform. That is why many mods have come up to copy what it offers. One of the many WhatsApp mods you can download today is JTWhatsapp. Unlike the original WhatsApp, this application provides users access to new and improved features that are not available in the latest version of WhatsApp. But although you can use it to communicate with people similar to WhatsApp, the app also comes with risks.

So, what is JTWhatsapp? Technically, it is a WhatsApp mod or a modified version of the original WhatsApp. However, unlike WhatsApp, it is not built from scratch. Instead, it changes WhatsApp’s source code and other WhatsApp mods.

That gives it the flexibility of adding more features unavailable on WhatsApp. For example, the mod allows you to share videos of up to 30MB, unlike WhatsApp, which only allows 16MB.

JTWhatsapp’s features are more comprehensive than original WhatsApp or other similar mods. Here is an in-depth look at its features:

Higher picture quality

When you send any image via the original WhatsApp, the application reduces the size and quality of the picture by up to 70% before the recipient can download it. The reason for doing so is to ensure faster data transmission. Also, it helps to reduce bandwidth usage and minimise the load on WhatsApp’s servers.

JTWhatsapp does not reduce the quality of an image. Instead, it maintains the quality of the pictures sent. So although the recipient gets a high-quality image, it can take time to download it.

JTWhatsapp has more themes

The mod has dozens of themes you can use to customise your messaging interface. Also, you can download themes from an online library, upload your preferred themes from an external device, create backups for your themes, and restore your theme to default.

File previews

JTWhatsapp displays a preview of a received photo, video, and other files. That allows you to determine whether it is worth downloading.

Support multiple file formats

A unique thing about JBWhatsapp is that you can download a file in almost any format. The supported file formats are PDF, DOC, DOCX, XLS, XLSX, TXT, PPT, PPTX, VCARD, RTF, and ZIP.

Hide profile picture

With JBWhatsapp, you can hide your contacts’ profile pictures. Also, you can show their pictures in the phonebook.

Share more photos simultaneously

JTWhatsapp allows you to share up to 90 photos simultaneously, compared to ten images other applications allow.

Freeze last seen

You can enable the last seen on JTWhatsapp. The option will freeze the Last Seen on your profile. Once you freeze it, your contacts will only view your Last Seen from the moment you enabled the option and not in future. However, you must restart the app to effect the changes.

How to install JTWhatsapp

You can install JTWhatsapp by first downloading the .apk file. However, you must be careful with the download source because not all sites have good intentions. But understand that the Android OS will block you from trying to install an app from an unknown source. Once you have the .apk file, proceed as follows:

  • Go to Settings.
  • Tap on Apps and Notifications.
  • Select Special App Access.
  • Scroll through the list and tap on Install Unknown Apps.
  • From the list provided, tap on any browser you have on your phone.
  • Toggle the slider Allow from this source.
  • Go back to the list and tap on File Manager.
  • Toggle the slider Allow from this source.
  • You must enable the slider to install JTWhatsapp on your device.

You could expose your personal information to third parties, which may result in identity theft or loss of your data, including your WhatsApp account.
