Jada Pinkett Smith revealed she left her and Will Smith's L.A. home in 2021. We look at the palatial pad she chose to leave

    Jada Pinkett Smith recently revealed that she and her husband, Will Smith, separated way back in 2016, sharing that she moved out of their home in 2021, relocating to Baltimore from Calabasas, where she lived with her family.

    It is believed that Will remained in the family home in California, a property reportedly worth an estimated $42million.

    Calabasas is a star-studded neighborhood, which counts the Kardashians among its other famous residents – hence why Will's son Jaden Smith was close to Kylie Jenner in childhood.

    The Smith estate was fully completed in 2010 and has some seriously impressive facilities, including basketball, volleyball and tennis courts, its very own lake, and an outdoor pool.

    Inside, the house has nine bedrooms, a meditation lounge, pool room, home theatre, and even a recording studio, where Willow reportedly recorded her hit singlewhen she was 10 years old.
