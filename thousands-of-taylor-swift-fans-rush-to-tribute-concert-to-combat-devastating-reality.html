'Thousands' of Taylor Swift fans rush to tribute concert to combat devastating reality

July 14, 2024
blog

Taylor Swift fans aka Swifties are proving once again, why Taytay is called the “music industry.”

In Swiftie Universe, 2023 has been a year worth the wait. From Taylor Swift‘s ‘Eras Tour’ to getting two Taylor Swift re-recorded albums in a single year could be a lot for early Swifties! But not all fans have been equally lucky, especially in their quest to secure an Eras Tour ticket. So the relatively ‘unlucky’ fans are finding their own means to create Swiftie-core memories and the turnout of crowds at Swiftie-special events can even compete with any real concert’s audience!

‘Thousands’ of Taylor Swift fans rush to tribute concert to combat devastating reality

After Taylor Swift’s record-breaking and history-making Eras tour leg in the U.S., Miss Americana has announced a list of European and Asian cities she will be performing next but the limited number of venues barely cover the entire globe leaving fans on their own to arrange some Swiftie events.

Heartbroken Philippine fans of Taylor Swift were one of the unfortunate ones who did not get a date on the Eras tour and they took it upon themselves to make the best of Swiftie world bringing approximately 10,000 fans to a tribute concert.

Fans still hoping for additional Eras tour dates

The viral video showing how a tribute to Swift concert gathered thousands of fans is making fans urge to Cruel Summer crooner that maybe it is time to add more stops to her Eras World Tour!

A fan posted: “Petition for real Taylor Swift to take the eras tour to the Philippines!” Another fan wrote: “she’s the greatest living artist and you can’t change my mind.”

“Now imagine if that was Taylor in the Philippines. This country is treating Taylor like a national hero. Would break records for sure,” a third fan announced.

Taylor Swift is coming to Singapore

While Philippines fans have to wait a bit longer to get a Taylor Swift show in the country, fans in Asia are looking forward to the epic leg of the Singapore tour of Taytay.

The Midnights crooner will be holding five shows back to back in Singapore in March 2024 on the 2nd, 3rd, 4th, 7th, 8th, and 9th at the Singapore National Stadium.
