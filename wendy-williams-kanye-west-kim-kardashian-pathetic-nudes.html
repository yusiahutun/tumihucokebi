Wendy Williams Rails on 'Unreasonable' Kanye West and Kim Kardashian's 'Pathetic' Nudes

February 25, 2024

Wendy Williams had a lot of thoughts to share about the Kardashian family this morning on her talk show, taking aim at the latest headlines surrounding Kanye West, Kim Kardashian and her mom Kris Jenner.

This is the second day in a row Wendy has called out Kanye, who she said is "continuing to spiral out of control" as his behavior continues to make the news.

First, she addressed reports that West broke ties with manager Scooter Braun. According to TMZ, West gave his management team an ultimatum: work with him full-time or not at all. Braun, who also manages the careers of Justin Bieber and Ariana Grande, wasn't having it. "If I were Scooter, I wouldn't fire Grande and all the other girls for a cuckoo," Wendy said. "Sorry, sorry, sorry, he's going through things. For him, I think this demand is unreasonable, but so is he under present conditions."

"When you hired Scooter, Kanye, you knew he had other clients," she added. "If you want to be managed by a boutique manager, find someone who can only focus on you."

She then moved on to a People magazine report saying he's had "explosive fights" with Kris, who was quick to deny on Twitter by saying, "Lies, Lies, Lies." She also responded to another report with the headline, "Kanye West 'Seems to Be on the Edge' and 'Is Very Hard to Deal with Right Now,'" by saying "Nope...not true!"

"I don't believe that," Wendy said of Kris' denials. "I do believe Kris did say to him you're ruining the brand. But I also believe that Kanye is helping ruin the brand, his own as well. We're looking at anther loose nut in the tree in the empire."

Williams shifted gears to Kanye's wife, Kim, and the fully nude photos she posted on Tuesday to promote her new fragrance. "I don't know whether this is just a boring day in her life or whether she's purposely doing this to draw attention away from the Kanye debacle, which has exploded," Williams said. "Yesterday she posts this picture nude, totally nude. Totally done, no razor bumps. No ingrown hairs."

Though she admitted the photos look "good," she couldn't resist adding, "But how pathetic is she? How pathetic."

Williams' latest rant comes after she previously said West hasn't "hasn't been the same" since his hospitalization in 2016, when he was treated for a mental breakdown. On Tuesday's show Williams added, "He hasn't been the same since he married Kim [Kardashian]."
