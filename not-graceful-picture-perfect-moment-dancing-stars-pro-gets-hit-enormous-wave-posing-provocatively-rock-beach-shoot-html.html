Dancing With the Stars's Brittany Cherry hit by enormous wave during a beach shoot

May 05, 2024
blog

The professional dancers on Dancing With the Stars have total control over their bodies, bending and moving in amazing ways —  but they don't have any control over Mother Nature.

That's something current DWTS and former So You Think You Can Dance pro Brittany Cherry learned the hard way on a recent beach photoshoot.

The 21-year-old was posing glamorously on a rock in the ocean when a wave came rumbling toward her, crashing into the rock and soaking her completely from head to toe.  

Glam work: Yesterday, Dancing With the Stars pro Brittany Cherry posted a video on Instagram from a beach photoshoot

Hold on... A photographer off-camera can be heard warning her that a wave is coming

Don't worry: Because it doesn't seem like a threat until it hits the rock, he just tells her to be 'very stoic'

She's got moves: Brittany has appeared on Dancing With the Stars and has also competed on So You Think You Can Dance

In an Instagram clip of the incident, Brittany is seen standing barefoot on the rock, wearing a black spaghetti-strap gown with a high slit.

She's lifting up the skirt of the dress and letting it down in a graceful move while photographers Derek and Drew Riker snap away.

Off-camera, one of the photographers can be heard saying that there's a wave coming up behind her — though he doesn't seem to be worried about it.

'Just be very stoic,' he advises her, and she seems to follow his instructions, standing in a ballet pose with one of her feet turned inward, holding her body tall and still.

Getting a little wet: However, the water comes crashing down around her

The big moment: She is totally obscured by the water as the video moves in slow-motion

Wow! After the wave washes over her, she remains balanced on the rock but completely soaked

That was fun: She shouts, 'Oh my god!' but seems to be a good sport about it

To be fair, the wave doesn't look too big as it's coming toward her — in fact, the rolling water comes up lower than the height of the rock.

But as it crashes into the rock, the wave explodes, creating a wall of water that crashes over her and soaks her from head to toe.

Brittany looks surprised — for the split second you can see her before she's engulfed by the water that is. Then, she's totally hidden, coming out on the other side of the wave absolutely drenched.

The dancer seems shocked, her black dress now wet and clinging to her as her hair drips around her shoulders. 

Just keep moving: Brittany has appeared on numerous dancing shows and was part of the Dancing With the Stars live tour

Graceful: She also starred in Ed Sheeran's music video for Thinking Out Loud

Shaking it off: The dancer shared the clip of herself getting wet with the caption: 'Well that was fun'

Impressively, she's managed to remain balanced on the rock, and after a second of recovery shouts: 'Oh my god!'

'Well that was fun,' she joked, captioning the short video.

She certainly seems to be good at keeping her composure — a must for someone who appears on live TV.

In addition to several performances on Dancing With the Stars, Brittany danced on the TV show's live tour and competed on season ten of So You Think You Can Dance. She was also in Ed Sheeran's music video for Thinking Out Loud.
