Ricky Martin: Nine of the singer's cutest moments with his four children

August 11, 2024
blog

Ricky Martin is a doting father to his four children, twins Matteo and Valentino, 14, daughter Lucia, four, and son Renn, three, and the singer has not been afraid of sharing his children with the world.

Although they don't always feature on his social media pages, Ricky has shared several insights into his life as a father-of-four as he proves how much his offspring mean to him. Over the years, they have joined him on outings, and he's shared heart-melting photos of the brood, whether they've been chilling on the beach, heading off on a trip together, or just playing around at home.

Join HELLO! as we take a look at nine of our favorite moments of the "Livin' la Vida Loca" hitmaker being the best father to his kids.

Earlier in the month, Ricky captured a relaxed moment at home, and in the video above you can see him giggling with son Matteo as they play with the family dogs. How sweet!

Ricky and daughter Lucia share the same birthday, and in 2021 they marked their joint celebration with a trip to the beach. The doting dad even captured a moment where his young girl ran into his arms before spinning her around.

The star has been thrilling fans lately as he's been taking his children with him to special events and he made a special appearance at the Grammy's in 2019 with son Mateo who supported his dad at the ceremony.

Like any parent, Ricky can't resist embarassing his children with signs of love as he proved in 2018 with a sweet kiss on Valentino's cheek, even if the youngster looked far from impressed with his father's show of affection.

What a cute photo! Ricky shared this shot in 2021 of him and one-year-old Renn having a sweet moment as they played together by the family's pool.

Back in 2019, the singer melted his fans' hearts when he shared this shot of Lucia after a bath all adorably wrapped up in a lion dressing gown. We still can't handle it!

Ricky and his sons are certainly lookalikes and Matteo took after his father as they posed together near baby Renn's crib.

Screen time is an issue in any family and while Ricky pulled a selection of funny faces behind daughter Lucia she didn't want to take her eyes off her tablet.

Back in 2019, Ricky shared the ultimate throwback when he looked back on ten years of his sons, Matteo and Valentino. Emulating the style of the times, Ricky had frosted tips as he lifted one of his boys out of their crib.
