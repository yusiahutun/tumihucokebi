Hop on the red shoe trend like Jessica Alba and Hailey Baldwin: 5 show-stopping pieces by Dior, Bale

June 27, 2024
blog

Dolce & Gabbana 

Made in Italy, this feminine and elegant Mary Jane is ideal for dinners and parties. Napa leather and the Baroque D&G heel make sure the shoe will stand out, HK$10,500 (US$1,350).

Balenciaga

Made of soft, wipe-clean, 100 per cent napa lambskin, this 80mm trumpet heel is finished with a special rounded open toe for a casual but elegant feel, HK$5,900.

Hermès

Made of goatskin with contrasting lining and palladium plated details, this mule’s vivid red is eye-catching indeed. The trendy red leather heel is topped with a Venice pink goatskin insole and lining, HK$8,300. 

YSL

These low pumps with a covered kitten heel and pointed toe are effortlessly feminine. Made of 100 per cent lambskin, they feature a flat double bow with YSL’s gold-tone metal monogram, HK$6,450.

Dior

These striking red patent calfskin ballet flats feature natural leather soles finished with a signature silver Dior logo, and are topped with a black-on-white woven bow bearing the brand’s name, HK$5,000.
