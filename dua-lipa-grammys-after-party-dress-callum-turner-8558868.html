Dua Lipa Arrived at a Grammys After-Party with Callum Turner

February 22, 2024
blog

Dua Lipa was working overtime last night at the 2024 Grammys. Not only did she open the show with a jaw-dropping performance (while debuting her single, "Training Season"), but she also brought her fashion A-game (as per usual) throughout the night. Lipa walked the carpet in a plunging silver fringe custom Courreges gown with ovary cutouts before slipping into a black leather bodysuit with mesh paneling when she took the stage.

Lipa closed out the show, and was seen arriving at the after-party, in a black long-sleeve backless dress with straps and buckles along the base of her back to resemble an exposed thong. While the whale tail was certainly eye-catching, it was her plus-one that had everyone talking. Although he skipped out on the ceremony, Lipa's new beau Callum Turner accompanied the pop star to the W Magazine, Mark Ronson, and Gucci Grammys after-party at the famed Chateau Marmont. The new couple held hands while climbing out of their car and into the rain.

Lipa kept her deep red hair styled in the middle part and beach waves that she rocked at the ceremony, and her glam included dewy skin, feathered lashes, and a subtle beige lip.

For his part, Turner looked dapper in a black tuxedo paired with a white button-up and long black skinny tie.

Although Turner didn't join her at the show, Lipa still had a very special date by her side. Her father Dukagjin Lipa walked with her along the carpet and the father-daughter duo even posed for a few photos together. Dukagjin proved that fashion runs in the family in a dashing black tux and bow tie.

Lipa didn't take home any awards last night, though the star was up for two Grammys: Song of the Year and Best Song Written for Visual Media for "Dance the Night (From the Barbie Album)."
