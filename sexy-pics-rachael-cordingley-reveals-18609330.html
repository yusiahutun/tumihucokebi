Knockout babe Rachael Cordingley was forced to quit ITV dance show Stepping Out after her boxer hubby and dance partner Carl Froch pulled out with a back injury.

But we've made up for Britain’s loss by showing as much of the 26-year-old stunner as possible in our red-hot snaps.

Rachael’s hubby Carl said he opted to quit the ITV dance contest so he could be in optimum condition for his forthcoming fight against George Groves.

A sad Rachael tweeted: “I really am gutted me & Carl Froch have had to pull out of Stepping Out due to a back injury. Thanks to everyone who voted for us x”

In private though, the sexpot model was less diplomatic about the pair’s exit from the contest, with Carl revealing she didn’t speak to him for two days after the decision to quit.

He said: “This leaves Rachel hating me. I think there’s a split-up on the cards! She’s not spoken to me for two days but what can I do about it?

“I’ve got to be selfish. Boxing is a selfish sport and I have got a genuine injury in my back. I can’t dance any more, it’s too much."

As if to rub salt in the wound, the unified super-middle weight champion even hinted that the injury to his back was caused by Rachael’s weight.

During the Davina McCall-fronted programme, Carl often lifted Rachael as part of their demanding routines.

The 36-year-old fighter said: "I’ve got a niggle in my back and I don’t want it to get any worse so I’ve done the professional thing and I’ve spoke to the ITV broadcasters and said look it’ll be better for me if I can pull out of this tournament with this injury.

“It’s not good being my feet six hours a day, twisting and turning lifting and lifting.

“They had me doing a lot of lifts because I’m big and I’m strong. They had me doing big lifts with Rachel picking her up above my head and spinning her around like a lunatic.”
