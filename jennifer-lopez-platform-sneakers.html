Jennifer Lopez's Off-Duty Look Includes White Platform Sneakers

April 22, 2024
blog

When she’s not in sky-high platform heels, Jennifer Lopez is in sky-high platform sneakers.

The superstar subtly championed the controversial shoe style on her latest outing in New York City, where she dressed like a true local in breezy all-black staples.

Her sneakers of choice this time around were K-Swiss’s Classic VN Platforms in bright white. (The tennis-inspired pair retails for just $75.) Lopez wore them with a black cropped waffle sweater and black flared leggings. To keep warm, she also layered on a black double-breasted coat.

K-Swiss Classic VN Platform

K-Swiss Classic VN Platform

To finish her off-duty look, the This Is Me…Now singer added her signature accessories: oversize black Dsquared2 sunglasses, huge gold hoop earrings, and a large black Hermès Birkin bag—which, as usual, she seemed to be using as a gym bag. Lopez had her hair up in a messy ponytail with wavy middle-parted bangs framing her face; she seemed to wear bronzy makeup and a glossy pink lip.

J.Lo is not the only star who has been spotted re-embracing the platform and chunky sneaker as of late. Shakira recently stepped out looking like a grungy ’90s teen in an old tee, big jeans, and super-high white platform sneakers.

Meanwhile, It girls like Katie Holmes, Hailey Bieber, and Gigi Hadid have been regularly stepping out in a variety of chunky sneakers—sometimes pairing them with a loose suit, athleisure, or hot shorts and micro miniskirts for a night-out look.
