The NHIF outpatient services were launched under the national scheme known as The Supa Cover. NHIF registered individuals are eligible for medical services covered by the fund. However, this only applies to the NHIF accredited health facilities. Mombasa residents were not left behind. There are numerous NHIF outpatient hospitals in Mombasa.

The National Hospital Insurance Fund (NHIF) provides medical insurance cover to Kenyan citizens. However, the individual must be a registered member to enjoy these benefits. After registration, the member and their declared dependents enjoy medical insurance cover whenever they need it.

NHIF outpatient hospitals in Mombasa

People living in Mombasa have the opportunity to enjoy NHIF medical cover at the comfort of their county as there are numerous NHIF accredited hospitals in the region. NHIF hospital codes in Mombasa helps one to identify his or her hospital of choice easily. Below is a list of Mombasa County NHIF outpatient hospitals.
